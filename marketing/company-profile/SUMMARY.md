## Prinsip Desain

### Show Don't Tell
- ✅ Gunakan visual, icons, charts
- ✅ Tables untuk data
- ✅ Bullet points daripada paragraf
- ❌ Hindari paragraf panjang
- ❌ Hindari essay-style writing

### Layout per Page
- **Header**: Judul + tagline singkat
- **Body**: Tables, bullet points, icons
- **Footer**: Source/attribution (minimal)

### Typography
- **Heading**: Bold, large, clear hierarchy
- **Body**: Readable size, good line height
- **Tables**: Clean, aligned, easy to scan

### White Space
- Jangan overcrowded
- Beri ruang napas antar section
- Balance text dan visual
