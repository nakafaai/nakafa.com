Given <InlineMath math="P(x) = x^3 - ax^2 - bx + 12" /> with factors <InlineMath math="(x - 1)" /> and <InlineMath math="(x + 3)" />

#### Finding Values of a and b

If the factor is <InlineMath math="x = 1" />, then <InlineMath math="P(1) = 0" />

<MathContainer>
<BlockMath math="(1)^3 - a(1)^2 - b(1) + 12 = 0" />

<BlockMath math="-a - b + 13 = 0" />

<BlockMath math="a + b = 13 \quad \text{...(i)}" />
</MathContainer>

If the factor is <InlineMath math="x = -3" />, then <InlineMath math="P(-3) = 0" />

<MathContainer>
<BlockMath math="(-3)^3 - a(-3)^2 - b(-3) + 12 = 0" />

<BlockMath math="-27 - 9a + 3b + 12 = 0" />

<BlockMath math="-9a + 3b = 15" />

<BlockMath math="3a - b = -5 \quad \text{...(ii)}" />
</MathContainer>

Eliminate equations <InlineMath math="\text{(i)}" /> and <InlineMath math="\text{(ii)}" />

<BlockMath math="\begin{array}{rcrcrl} 3a & - & b & = & -5 & \\ a & + & b & = & 13 & + \\ \hline 4a & & & = & 8 & \\ a & & & = & 2 & \end{array}" />

Substitute the value of <InlineMath math="a" />

<MathContainer>
<BlockMath math="a + b = 13" />

<BlockMath math="2 + b = 13 \Rightarrow b = 11" />
</MathContainer>

Therefore <InlineMath math="P(x) = x^3 - 2x^2 - 11x + 12" />

#### Finding Other Factors Using Horner

Other factors of <InlineMath math="P(x)" /> are found using Horner with <InlineMath math="(x - 1) \Rightarrow x = 1" />

<BlockMath math="\begin{array}{r|rrrrl} & x^3 & x^2 & x^1 & x^0 & \\ \hline 1 & 1 & -2 & -11 & 12 & \\ & & 1 & -1 & -12 & + \\ \hline & 1 & -1 & -12 & 0 & \rightarrow S(x) \\ & x^2 & x^1 & x^0 & & \end{array}" />

The division result is <InlineMath math="S(x) = x^2 - x - 12" />

Factor <InlineMath math="x^2 - x - 12" />

<BlockMath math="x^2 - x - 12 = (x - 4)(x + 3)" />

Therefore, the factor besides <InlineMath math="(x - 1)" /> and <InlineMath math="(x + 3)" /> is <InlineMath math="(x - 4)" />

#### Determining Sum of Roots

Since <InlineMath math="x_1 < x_2 < x_3" />, then

<MathContainer>
<BlockMath math="x_1 = -3, \quad x_2 = 1, \quad x_3 = 4" />

<BlockMath math="x_1 + x_2 + x_3 = -3 + 1 + 4 = 2" />
</MathContainer>

Therefore, the value of <InlineMath math="x_1 + x_2 + x_3 = 2" />
