To solve <InlineMath math="\sin 75^\circ - \sin 165^\circ" />, use the sine difference formula

<BlockMath math="\sin A - \sin B = 2\cos\frac{1}{2}(A + B)\sin\frac{1}{2}(A - B)" />

#### Applying the Formula

Substitute <InlineMath math="A = 75^\circ" /> and <InlineMath math="B = 165^\circ" />

<MathContainer>
<BlockMath math="\sin 75^\circ - \sin 165^\circ = 2\cos\frac{1}{2}(75^\circ + 165^\circ)\sin\frac{1}{2}(75^\circ - 165^\circ)" />

<BlockMath math="= 2\cos 120^\circ \sin(-45^\circ)" />
</MathContainer>

#### Simplifying

Use the identities <InlineMath math="\cos 120^\circ = \cos(180^\circ - 60^\circ) = -\cos 60^\circ" /> and <InlineMath math="\sin(-45^\circ) = -\sin 45^\circ" />

<MathContainer>
<BlockMath math="= 2 \cdot \cos(180^\circ - 60^\circ) \cdot (-\sin 45^\circ)" />

<BlockMath math="= 2 \cdot (-\cos 60^\circ) \cdot (-\sin 45^\circ)" />

<BlockMath math="= 2\cos 60^\circ \sin 45^\circ" />
</MathContainer>

Substitute values <InlineMath math="\cos 60^\circ = \frac{1}{2}" /> and <InlineMath math="\sin 45^\circ = \frac{1}{2}\sqrt{2}" />

<MathContainer>
<BlockMath math="= 2 \cdot \left(\frac{1}{2}\right) \cdot \left(\frac{1}{2}\sqrt{2}\right)" />

<BlockMath math="= 1 \cdot \frac{1}{2}\sqrt{2}" />

<BlockMath math="= \frac{1}{2}\sqrt{2}" />
</MathContainer>

Therefore, the value of <InlineMath math="\sin 75^\circ - \sin 165^\circ = \frac{1}{2}\sqrt{2}" />
