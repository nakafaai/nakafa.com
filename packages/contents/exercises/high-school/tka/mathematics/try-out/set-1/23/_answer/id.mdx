#### Perhatikan Segitiga TQR

Hitung panjang <InlineMath math="TR" /> menggunakan teorema Pythagoras pada segitiga siku-siku di alas

<MathContainer>
<BlockMath math="TR = \sqrt{QR^2 + QT^2}" />

<BlockMath math="= \sqrt{3^2 + 3^2}" />

<BlockMath math="= \sqrt{18} = 3\sqrt{2}" />
</MathContainer>

#### Menghitung TO

Karena <InlineMath math="O" /> adalah pusat alas, maka <InlineMath math="TO" /> adalah setengah dari diagonal <InlineMath math="TR" />

<MathContainer>
<BlockMath math="TO = \frac{1}{2}TR" />

<BlockMath math="= \frac{1}{2} \cdot 3\sqrt{2}" />

<BlockMath math="= \frac{3}{2}\sqrt{2}" />
</MathContainer>

#### Menghitung PO

Gunakan teorema Pythagoras pada segitiga <InlineMath math="PTO" />

<MathContainer>
<BlockMath math="PO = \sqrt{PR^2 - RO^2}" />

<BlockMath math="= \sqrt{(3\sqrt{2})^2 - \left(\frac{3}{2}\sqrt{2}\right)^2}" />

<BlockMath math="= \sqrt{18 - \frac{9}{2}}" />

<BlockMath math="= \sqrt{\frac{36 - 9}{2}} = \sqrt{\frac{27}{2}}" />
</MathContainer>

#### Menghitung Tangen Sudut

Tangen sudut <InlineMath math="\alpha" /> antara garis PT dan alas QRST

<MathContainer>
<BlockMath math="\tan \alpha = \frac{PO}{TO}" />

<BlockMath math="= \frac{\sqrt{\frac{27}{2}}}{\frac{3}{2}\sqrt{2}}" />

<BlockMath math="= \frac{\sqrt{27}}{\sqrt{2}} \cdot \frac{1}{\frac{3}{2}\sqrt{2}}" />

<BlockMath math="= \frac{\sqrt{27}}{\sqrt{2}} \cdot \frac{2}{3\sqrt{2}}" />

<BlockMath math="= \frac{2\sqrt{27}}{3 \cdot 2}" />

<BlockMath math="= \frac{\sqrt{27}}{3} = \frac{3\sqrt{3}}{3} = \sqrt{3}" />
</MathContainer>

Jadi, tangen sudut antara garis PT dan alas QRST adalah <InlineMath math="\sqrt{3}" />
