To simplify <InlineMath math="\frac{3\sqrt{3} + \sqrt{7}}{\sqrt{7} - 2\sqrt{3}}" />, we rationalize the denominator by multiplying both the numerator and denominator by the conjugate of the denominator, which is <InlineMath math="\sqrt{7} + 2\sqrt{3}" />

<MathContainer>
<BlockMath math="\frac{3\sqrt{3} + \sqrt{7}}{\sqrt{7} - 2\sqrt{3}} = \frac{3\sqrt{3} + \sqrt{7}}{\sqrt{7} - 2\sqrt{3}} \times \frac{\sqrt{7} + 2\sqrt{3}}{\sqrt{7} + 2\sqrt{3}}" />

<BlockMath math="= \frac{(3\sqrt{3} + \sqrt{7})(\sqrt{7} + 2\sqrt{3})}{(\sqrt{7} - 2\sqrt{3})(\sqrt{7} + 2\sqrt{3})}" />

<BlockMath math="= \frac{3\sqrt{3} \cdot \sqrt{7} + 3\sqrt{3} \cdot 2\sqrt{3} + \sqrt{7} \cdot \sqrt{7} + \sqrt{7} \cdot 2\sqrt{3}}{(\sqrt{7})^2 - (2\sqrt{3})^2}" />

<BlockMath math="= \frac{3\sqrt{21} + 6 \cdot 3 + 7 + 2\sqrt{21}}{7 - 4 \cdot 3}" />

<BlockMath math="= \frac{3\sqrt{21} + 18 + 7 + 2\sqrt{21}}{7 - 12}" />

<BlockMath math="= \frac{25 + 5\sqrt{21}}{-5}" />

<BlockMath math="= \frac{25}{-5} + \frac{5\sqrt{21}}{-5}" />

<BlockMath math="= -5 - \sqrt{21}" />
</MathContainer>
