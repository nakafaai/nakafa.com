Let the first profit <InlineMath math="U_1 = a = 46{,}000" /> and the increase <InlineMath math="b = 18{,}000" />

#### Determining Total Profit

The formula for the sum of the first <InlineMath math="n" /> terms of an arithmetic series

<BlockMath math="S_n = \frac{n}{2}(2a + (n - 1)b)" />

For <InlineMath math="n = 12" />, substitute the known values

<MathContainer>
<BlockMath math="S_{12} = \frac{12}{2}(2(46{,}000) + (12 - 1)18{,}000)" />

<BlockMath math="= 6(92{,}000 + 11 \cdot 18{,}000)" />

<BlockMath math="= 6(92{,}000 + 198{,}000)" />

<BlockMath math="= 6(290{,}000)" />

<BlockMath math="= 1{,}740{,}000" />
</MathContainer>

Therefore, the total profit up to the 12th month is <InlineMath math="\text{Rp}1{,}740{,}000" />
