Untuk menyelesaikan integral tentu <InlineMath math="\int_1^2 (4x^2 - x + 5) dx" />, kita integralkan terlebih dahulu

#### Mengintegralkan

<MathContainer>
<BlockMath math="\int_1^2 (4x^2 - x + 5) dx = \left[\frac{4}{3}x^3 - \frac{1}{2}x^2 + 5x\right]_1^2" />
</MathContainer>

#### Substitusi Batas Atas dan Bawah

Substitusi <InlineMath math="x = 2" /> dan <InlineMath math="x = 1" />

<MathContainer>
<BlockMath math="= \left(\frac{4}{3}(2)^3 - \frac{1}{2}(2)^2 + 5(2)\right) - \left(\frac{4}{3}(1)^3 - \frac{1}{2}(1)^2 + 5(1)\right)" />

<BlockMath math="= \left(\frac{4}{3} \cdot 8 - \frac{1}{2} \cdot 4 + 10\right) - \left(\frac{4}{3} - \frac{1}{2} + 5\right)" />

<BlockMath math="= \left(\frac{32}{3} - 2 + 10\right) - \left(\frac{4}{3} - \frac{1}{2} + 5\right)" />

<BlockMath math="= \left(\frac{32}{3} + 8\right) - \left(\frac{4}{3} - \frac{1}{2} + 5\right)" />
</MathContainer>

#### Menyederhanakan

<MathContainer>
<BlockMath math="= \frac{32}{3} + 8 - \frac{4}{3} + \frac{1}{2} - 5" />

<BlockMath math="= \frac{32 - 4}{3} + 8 - 5 + \frac{1}{2}" />

<BlockMath math="= \frac{28}{3} + 3 + \frac{1}{2}" />

<BlockMath math="= \frac{56}{6} + \frac{18}{6} + \frac{3}{6}" />

<BlockMath math="= \frac{56 + 18 + 3}{6} = \frac{77}{6}" />
</MathContainer>

Jadi, nilai dari <InlineMath math="\int_1^2 (4x^2 - x + 5) dx = \frac{77}{6}" />
