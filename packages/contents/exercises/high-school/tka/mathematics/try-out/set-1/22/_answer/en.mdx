#### Determining Face Diagonal

If <InlineMath math="AC" /> and <InlineMath math="PR" /> are face diagonals, then

<BlockMath math="AC = PR = 12\sqrt{2}" />

#### Calculating Distance from Point P to Line HB

Since <InlineMath math="P" /> is the midpoint of <InlineMath math="CG" /> and <InlineMath math="O" /> is the center of the cube, then

<MathContainer>
<BlockMath math="PO = \frac{1}{2}PR" />

<BlockMath math="= \frac{1}{2} \cdot 12\sqrt{2}" />

<BlockMath math="= 6\sqrt{2}" />
</MathContainer>

Therefore, the distance from point <InlineMath math="P" /> to line <InlineMath math="HB" /> is <InlineMath math="6\sqrt{2}" /> cm
