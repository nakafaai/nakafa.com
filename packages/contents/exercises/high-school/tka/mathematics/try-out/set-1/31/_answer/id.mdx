Jika integral parsial <InlineMath math="\int u \cdot dv = u \cdot v - \int v \, du" />, maka <InlineMath math="\int 2x(5 - x)^3 dx" />

#### Dimisalkan

Misalkan

<MathContainer>
<BlockMath math="u = 2x \Rightarrow du = 2dx" />

<BlockMath math="dv = (5 - x)^3 dx \Rightarrow v = \int dv = -\frac{1}{4}(5 - x)^4" />
</MathContainer>

#### Menerapkan Rumus Integral Parsial

Sehingga

<MathContainer>
<BlockMath math="\int u \cdot dv = 2x \left(-\frac{1}{4}(5 - x)^4\right) - \int \left(-\frac{1}{4}(5 - x)^4\right) 2dx" />

<BlockMath math="= \left(-\frac{x}{2}(5 - x)^4\right) + \frac{1}{2}\left(-\frac{1}{5}(5 - x)^5\right) + C" />

<BlockMath math="= -\frac{x}{2}(5 - x)^4 - \frac{1}{10}(5 - x)^5 + C" />
</MathContainer>

#### Menyederhanakan

Faktorkan <InlineMath math="(5 - x)^4" />

<MathContainer>
<BlockMath math="= \left(\frac{-x}{2} - \frac{(5 - x)}{10}\right)(5 - x)^4 + C" />

<BlockMath math="= \left(\frac{-5x - (5 - x)}{10}\right)(5 - x)^4 + C" />

<BlockMath math="= \left(\frac{-5x - 5 + x}{10}\right)(5 - x)^4 + C" />

<BlockMath math="= -\frac{1}{10}(4x + 5)(5 - x)^4 + C" />
</MathContainer>

Jadi, hasil dari <InlineMath math="\int 2x(5 - x)^3 dx = -\frac{1}{10}(4x + 5)(5 - x)^4 + C" />
