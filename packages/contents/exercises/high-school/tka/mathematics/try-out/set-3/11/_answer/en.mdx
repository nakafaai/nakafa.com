Let <InlineMath math="y = x - \frac{\pi}{2}" /> then <InlineMath math="x = y + \frac{\pi}{2}" />. Solve the limit with the above substitution.

<MathContainer>
<BlockMath math="= \lim_{y \to 0} \frac{\sec 2\left(y + \frac{\pi}{2}\right) + 2}{\tan 2\left(y + \frac{\pi}{2}\right)}" />

<BlockMath math="= \lim_{y \to 0} \frac{-\sec 2y + 2}{\tan 2y}" />

<BlockMath math="= \lim_{y \to 0} \frac{-\frac{1}{\cos 2y} + 2}{\frac{\sin 2y}{\cos 2y}}" />

<BlockMath math="= \lim_{y \to 0} \frac{\frac{-1 + 2 \cos 2y}{\cos 2y}}{\frac{\sin 2y}{\cos 2y}}" />

<BlockMath math="= \lim_{y \to 0} \frac{-1 + 2 \cos 2y}{\sin 2y}" />
</MathContainer>
