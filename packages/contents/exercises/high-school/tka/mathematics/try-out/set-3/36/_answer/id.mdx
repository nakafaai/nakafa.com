Tentukan turunan fungsinya

<MathContainer>
<BlockMath math="y = 4 \sin x \sin(x - 60°)" />

<BlockMath math="y' = 4[\cos x \cdot \sin(x - 60°) + \sin x \cdot \cos(x - 60°)]" />
</MathContainer>

Gunakan identitas trigonometri <InlineMath math="\sin A \cos B + \cos A \sin B = \sin(A + B)" /> dan <InlineMath math="\cos A \cos B - \sin A \sin B = \cos(A + B)" />

<MathContainer>
<BlockMath math="y' = 4[\sin(x - 60°) \cos x + \cos(x - 60°) \sin x]" />

<BlockMath math="y' = 4 \sin[(x - 60°) + x]" />

<BlockMath math="y' = 4 \sin(2x - 60°)" />
</MathContainer>

Atau dengan menggunakan identitas <InlineMath math="\sin A \sin B = \frac{1}{2}[\cos(A - B) - \cos(A + B)]" />

<MathContainer>
<BlockMath math="y = 4 \sin x \sin(x - 60°)" />

<BlockMath math="y = 4 \cdot \frac{1}{2}[\cos(x - (x - 60°)) - \cos(x + (x - 60°))]" />

<BlockMath math="y = 2[\cos(60°) - \cos(2x - 60°)]" />

<BlockMath math="y = 2 \cos 60° - 2 \cos(2x - 60°)" />

<BlockMath math="y' = -2 \cdot (-\sin(2x - 60°)) \cdot 2" />

<BlockMath math="y' = 4 \sin(2x - 60°)" />
</MathContainer>

Nilai maksimum terjadi saat <InlineMath math="y' = 0" /> atau saat <InlineMath math="\sin(2x - 60°)" /> mencapai nilai maksimumnya, yaitu <InlineMath math="\sin(2x - 60°) = 1" />.

Namun, karena <InlineMath math="y = 2 \cos 60° - 2 \cos(2x - 60°) = 1 - 2 \cos(2x - 60°)" />, maka nilai maksimum terjadi saat <InlineMath math="\cos(2x - 60°)" /> minimum, yaitu <InlineMath math="\cos(2x - 60°) = -1" />.

<MathContainer>
<BlockMath math="\cos(2x - 60°) = -1" />

<BlockMath math="2x - 60° = 180° + k \cdot 360°" />

<BlockMath math="2x = 240° + k \cdot 360°" />

<BlockMath math="x = 120° + k \cdot 180°" />
</MathContainer>

Dengan <InlineMath math="k" /> bilangan bulat.
