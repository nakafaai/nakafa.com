Let's calculate the percentage decrease in price for each given option. The formula for percentage decrease is:

<MathContainer>
  <BlockMath math="\text{Percentage Decrease} = \frac{\text{Initial Price} - \text{Final Price}}{\text{Initial Price}} \times 100\%" />
</MathContainer>

#### Analysis of Answer Choices

    *   Price 2019: <InlineMath math="3350" />

    *   Decrease: <InlineMath math="3350 - 3000 = 350" />

    <MathContainer>
      <BlockMath math="\frac{350}{3350} \times 100\% \approx 10.4\%" />
    </MathContainer>
