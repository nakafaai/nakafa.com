To determine the year with the largest percentage profit, we need to calculate the percentage profit for each year using the formula:

<MathContainer>
  <BlockMath math="\text{Percentage Profit} = \frac{\text{Revenue} - \text{Capital}}{\text{Capital}} \times 100\%" />
</MathContainer>

Here are the calculations for each year:

**Year 1**

<MathContainer>
  <BlockMath math="\frac{150 - 50}{50} \times 100\% = \frac{100}{50} \times 100\% = 200\%" />
</MathContainer>

**Year 2**

<MathContainer>
  <BlockMath math="\frac{200 - 100}{100} \times 100\% = \frac{100}{100} \times 100\% = 100\%" />
</MathContainer>

**Year 3**

<MathContainer>
  <BlockMath math="\frac{250 - 100}{100} \times 100\% = \frac{150}{100} \times 100\% = 150\%" />
</MathContainer>

**Year 4**

<MathContainer>
  <BlockMath math="\frac{250 - 150}{150} \times 100\% = \frac{100}{150} \times 100\% \approx 66.67\%" />
</MathContainer>

**Year 5**

<MathContainer>
  <BlockMath math="\frac{300 - 200}{200} \times 100\% = \frac{100}{200} \times 100\% = 50\%" />
</MathContainer>

Based on the calculations above, the largest percentage profit was obtained in **Year 1**, which is <InlineMath math="200\%" />.
