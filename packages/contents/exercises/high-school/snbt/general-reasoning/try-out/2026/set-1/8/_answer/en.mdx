To calculate the expenditure on agricultural production facilities and infrastructure, we need to use the percentage information found in the text.

Given:
1.  Total expenditure on agricultural production facilities and infrastructure reached the range of <InlineMath math="70\text{ percent}" /> of the total Ministry of Agriculture budget.

<MathContainer>
