Changing the meaning of a word to be more polite or courteous is called amelioration.

In the text, the word that experiences amelioration is *gugur* (fell). The word *gugur* is used as a more refined and honorable form to replace the word *mati* (died) or *tewas* (killed), especially when referring to heroes or fighters.
