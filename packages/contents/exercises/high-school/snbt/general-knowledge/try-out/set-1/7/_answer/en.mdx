The inappropriate use of diction (word choice) in the text is found in sentence <InlineMath math="(6)" />, specifically the word *penelitian* (research).

In that sentence, there is the phrase "tim penelitian internasional" (international research team). In Indonesian, the word *penelitian* refers to the activity or process of researching. If combined into "tim penelitian", the meaning becomes less logical within the context of the sentence because the subject capable of performing the action of "sequencing the genome" is a person or an actor, not the activity itself.

Therefore, the word *penelitian* (research) should be replaced with the word **peneliti** (researcher), making the phrase "tim peneliti internasional" (international team of researchers), which correctly denotes a team consisting of researchers from various countries.
