The phrase *warga lokal* (local residents) in sentence <InlineMath math="(6)" /> follows the Head-Modifier (D-M) pattern where the core of the phrase is a noun (*warga*), while its attribute is an adjective (*lokal*). The phrase that has the same pattern, which is a noun followed by an adjective, is *dataran tinggi* (plateau) because it consists of a core noun (*dataran*) and an adjective attribute (*tinggi*).
