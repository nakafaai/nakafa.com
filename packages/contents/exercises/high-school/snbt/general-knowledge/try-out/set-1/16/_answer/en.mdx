The meaning of the affix *ter-* in the word *terbatas* (limited) indicates a state or means "has been" (has been limited).

The word that has the same affix meaning is *terikat* (tied), which indicates the state of "has been tied". As a comparison, the words *terinjak* (stepped on) and *tertidur* (fell asleep) indicate an accidental action, while the words *terbaik* (best) and *termuda* (youngest) indicate the meaning of "most" (superlative).
