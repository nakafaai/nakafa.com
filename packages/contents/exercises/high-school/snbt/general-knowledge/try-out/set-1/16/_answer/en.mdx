The grammatical meaning of the word *limited* indicates a state or condition (a state of being limited).

The word that has the same grammatical meaning is *tied*, which indicates the state of being tied. As a comparison, the words *stepped on* and *fell asleep* indicate an accidental action, while the words *best* and *youngest* indicate a superlative meaning ("most").
