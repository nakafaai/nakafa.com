An antonym is a word that has the opposite meaning of another word.

In sentence <InlineMath math="(9)" />, the word *awake* (*terjaga*) means "in a waking state" or "not sleeping". Therefore, the most appropriate opposite meaning for the word *awake* in the context of the sentence is falling fast asleep or *asleep* (*terlelap*).
