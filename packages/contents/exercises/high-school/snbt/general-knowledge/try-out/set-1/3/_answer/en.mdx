The conjunction *however* in sentence <InlineMath math="(8)" /> should be replaced with the conjunction *but* because the conjunction *however* is an inter-sentence conjunction, whereas the usage in that sentence requires an intra-sentence conjunction that expresses contrast, which is *but*.

Therefore, the appropriate conjunction to replace *however* is **but**.
