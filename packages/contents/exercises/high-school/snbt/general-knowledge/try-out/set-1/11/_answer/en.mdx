A synonym or corresponding word is a word that has a different form but has the same or similar meaning.

In the text, specifically in sentence <InlineMath math="(4)" />, there is the phrase *kesimpangsiuran informasi* (confusing information). Lexically, the root word *simpang siur* refers to a situation that is crisscrossing randomly, tangled randomly, or having many complicated intricacies.

In the context of that sentence, *kesimpangsiuran* is used to describe the condition of information circulating in society that is confusing, contradictory, and uncertain in its truth. This uncertain and confusing condition has a meaning that aligns with an **unclarity** (ketidakjelasan).

Therefore, the most appropriate corresponding word to replace the word *kesimpangsiuran* in the text is *ketidakjelasan* (unclarity).
