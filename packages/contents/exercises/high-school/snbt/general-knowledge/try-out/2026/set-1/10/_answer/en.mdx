An antonym is a word that has the opposite meaning of another word.

In sentence <InlineMath math="(1)" />, the word *increasing* is used to describe the trend of sports going up or becoming more frequent during the pandemic. The opposite of a condition that is going up or increasing is a condition that is going down or decreasing. Therefore, the most appropriate antonym for the word *increasing* in the context of the sentence is *decrease*.

Words like *growth* and *progressive* actually have a similar meaning, while *climbing* and *rising* are less appropriate when used as exact opposites in this context.
