Reference words are used to refer back to something that has been mentioned previously to avoid repetition.

In the text, sentence <InlineMath math="(1)" /> explains the habits of **bats** that fly at night to hunt insects and small animals. Therefore, the reference word *they* in sentence <InlineMath math="(2)" /> clearly refers back to **bats**, which is the main subject in the previous sentence.
