The correct statement that corresponds to the text is: **"The 1,400-year-old genetic information plays an important role because it teaches us about the evolutionary history of the variola virus that causes smallpox."**

This is explicitly stated in the last sentence of the second paragraph (sentence 10): *"Furthermore, researchers stated that the 1,400-year-old genetic information is highly important because it teaches us about the evolutionary history of the variola virus that causes smallpox."*

Why are the other statements incorrect?
- The text mentions the DNA sample is the **earliest** dating back to 603 BCE (sentence 4), not the most recent.
- The text actually states that traces of the smallpox disease are **beginning to appear** (sentence 2), contradicting the claim that they are no longer visible.
- The text states the origin of smallpox emerged **at least** 1,400 years earlier (sentence 5), rather than "more than".
