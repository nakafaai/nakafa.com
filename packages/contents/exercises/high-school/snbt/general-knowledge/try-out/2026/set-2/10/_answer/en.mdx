The word *intolerant* means lacking tolerance or being unwilling to tolerate something. In a broader context, an intolerant attitude shows an unwillingness to accept circumstances, differences, or certain groups.

From the available answer choices, the word that has an equivalent meaning or conceptual proximity to *intolerant* is **discriminatory**. A discriminatory attitude also refers to treating things differently in an unfair manner or not tolerating a certain thing.
