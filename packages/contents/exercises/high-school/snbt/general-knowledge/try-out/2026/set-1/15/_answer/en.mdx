The phrase *local residents* in sentence <InlineMath math="(6)" /> consists of an adjective (*local*) followed by a core noun (*residents*). The phrase that has the same pattern, which is an adjective followed by a noun, is *high plateau* because it consists of an adjective attribute (*high*) and a core noun (*plateau*).
