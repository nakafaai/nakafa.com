A figure of speech that compares inanimate objects as if they were alive and could behave like humans is called personification.

In the text, the use of personification is clearly visible in sentence (6), namely in the sentence "Ten large bullets would dance into the sky within one second."

The word "dance" is an action or behavior generally performed by humans. However, in that sentence, this characteristic is attached to "large bullets" which are basically inanimate objects.
