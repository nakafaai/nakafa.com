Based on the dictionary definition, a **predator** refers to an animal that lives by preying on other animals.

In the context of the text, this is emphasized in sentence <InlineMath math="(12)" /> which gives the example of "birds of prey" as part of predators. Therefore, the most appropriate meaning of the word *predators* is **animals that prey on other animals**.
