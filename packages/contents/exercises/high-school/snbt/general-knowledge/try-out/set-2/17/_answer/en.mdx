A cohesive and complete sentence must at least have core elements in the form of a subject and a predicate.

Sentence <InlineMath math="(6)" /> is an incomplete sentence because it is missing a core element, namely a predicate. The phrase "The weeks that had already passed" syntactically only occupies one functional role (as a subject or noun phrase) without explaining what actually happened or was being done.
