Changing a word to a more polite or refined synonym is known as semantic amelioration or euphemism.

In the text, the phrase deliberately changed by the author to sound softer is *tubuhnya menipis* (his body was thinning). The use of the word *menipis* in that phrase is a refined form used to describe a person's physical condition, where the original intended meaning is *tubuhnya menjadi kurus* (his body was getting skinny/bony).
