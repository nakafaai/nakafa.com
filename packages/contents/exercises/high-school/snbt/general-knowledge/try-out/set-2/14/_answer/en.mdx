The phrase *gigitan serangga* (insect bites) is formed from two words, namely *gigitan* (bites) and *serangga* (insect). The word *gigitan* is a noun derived from the verb *gigit* (to bite) with the suffix *-an*, while *serangga* is also a noun. So, the forming pattern of the phrase is **noun + noun**.

Let's break down the phrases in the answer choices to find the same pattern:
- *temuan inovatif* (innovative findings): noun + adjective
- *timbangan berkarat* (rusty scales): noun + adjective
- *latihan menulis* (writing exercises): noun + verb
- *kicauan burung* (bird chirps): noun + noun (the word *kicauan* is a noun, as is *burung*)
- *tulisan sederhana* (simple writings): noun + adjective

Therefore, the phrase that has the exact same forming pattern as *gigitan serangga* is the phrase *kicauan burung* (bird chirps).
