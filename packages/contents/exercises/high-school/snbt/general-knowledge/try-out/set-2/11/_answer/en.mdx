Based on the dictionary, the word *fermentation* (peragian) means the process of breaking down organic compounds metabolically by microorganisms, which generally takes place in anaerobic conditions (without oxygen).

Therefore, the meaning of the phrase *susu fermentasi* (fermented milk) in the text specifically refers to **milk that has undergone fermentation** (peragian) by certain microorganisms. Examples of the results of this milk fermentation are *yoghurt*, *kefir*, and *koumis* as mentioned in sentence <InlineMath math="(6)" />.
