A figure of speech that states something excessively and is not in accordance with actual reality is called hyperbole.

In the text, the sentence that exaggerates the situation is sentence <InlineMath math="(4)" />. This can be seen in the phrase "brightly shining glint in his eyes". Literally, human eyes cannot shine brightly like a lamp. This phrase is an exaggerated figure of speech (hyperbole) used to describe a very enthusiastic or passionate look in the eyes.
