The meaning of the suffix *-an* in the word *gigitan* (bite) means 'result' (the result of the biting process).

Therefore, the word that has the exact same suffix meaning as that word is *tulisan* (writing — the result of the writing process). Words in the other choices have different meanings for the suffix *-an*, such as *ruangan* (place/room) or *panggilan* (the thing being called/call).
