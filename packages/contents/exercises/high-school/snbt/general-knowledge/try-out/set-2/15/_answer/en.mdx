The word *bites* is a noun that represents the 'result' of an action (the result of the biting process).

Therefore, the word that has the exact same grammatical meaning is *writing* (the result of the writing process). Words in the other choices represent different concepts, such as *room* (a place) or *call* (the act of calling).
