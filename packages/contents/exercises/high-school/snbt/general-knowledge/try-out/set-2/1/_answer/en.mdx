An antonym is a word whose meaning is the opposite of another word.

In sentence <InlineMath math="(1)" />, the word *increase* means to make more (larger, broader, etc.). In the context of the sentence, the most appropriate opposite word or antonym for the word *increase* is *reduce*, which means to make less.
