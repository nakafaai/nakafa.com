The word *accumulation* in sentence <InlineMath math="(5)" /> means the process, method, or act of piling up (collecting, stacking, or making something increase in quantity).

The antonym or opposite of the act of stacking or collecting something until full (accumulation) is the process of emptying or taking out its contents, which is **draining**.
