Based on the exposition in the text, there are various obstacles that cause fisheries production in Indonesia to be suboptimal. These obstacles include the large number of traditional fishermen with low catch yields, the unbalanced utilization of fish stocks between regions (management issues), and the low knowledge of aquaculture technology.

Therefore, a highly necessary comprehensive solution to maximize fisheries production is to improve human resources (including extension workers and fishermen), improve overall fisheries management, and improve the utilization of aquaculture technology.
