Sentence <InlineMath math="(3)" /> uses a correlative conjunction (paired conjunctions). In the sentence, there is the phrase *tidak hanya* (not only) which is always paired with the conjunction *tetapi (juga)* (but (also)).

For comparison, if the sentence uses the word *bukan (hanya)* (not (only)), the pair would be *melainkan (juga)* (but rather (also)). Because the sentence uses the word *tidak*, the appropriate conjunction to complete the blank is **tetapi** (but).
