Sentence <InlineMath math="(3)" /> uses a correlative conjunction (paired conjunctions). In the sentence, there is the phrase *not only* which is always paired with the conjunction *but (also)*.

For comparison, if the sentence uses the word *not (only)*, the pair would be *but rather (also)*. Because the sentence uses the word *not*, the appropriate conjunction to complete the blank is **but**.
