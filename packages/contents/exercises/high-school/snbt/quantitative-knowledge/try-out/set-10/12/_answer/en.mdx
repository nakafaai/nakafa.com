Given:

<MathContainer>
  <BlockMath math="33 \text{ workers} \rightarrow 21 \text{ days}" />
  <BlockMath math="14 \text{ workers} \rightarrow x \text{ days}" />
</MathContainer>

Using inverse proportion:

<MathContainer>
  <BlockMath math="\frac{33}{14} = \frac{x}{21}" />
</MathContainer>

<MathContainer>
  <BlockMath math="14x = 33 \times 21" />
</MathContainer>

<MathContainer>
  <BlockMath math="14x = 693" />
</MathContainer>

<MathContainer>
  <BlockMath math="x = \frac{693}{14}" />
</MathContainer>

<MathContainer>
  <BlockMath math="x = 49.5" />
</MathContainer>

Thus, they can complete the job in <InlineMath math="49.5" /> days.
