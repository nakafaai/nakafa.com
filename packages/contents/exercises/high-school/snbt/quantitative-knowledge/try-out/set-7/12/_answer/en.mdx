Given the operation rule:
<MathContainer>
  <BlockMath math="\beta(w \oplus x \ominus y \otimes z) = 2w + x \cdot y - (-z)" />
</MathContainer>

We are asked to find the value of:
<MathContainer>
  <BlockMath math="\beta(2 \oplus 3 \ominus 4 \otimes (-2))" />
</MathContainer>

By comparing variable positions, we get:
- <InlineMath math="w = 2" />
- <InlineMath math="x = 3" />
- <InlineMath math="y = 4" />
- <InlineMath math="z = -2" />

Substitute these values into the formula:
<MathContainer>
  <BlockMath math="\beta(2 \oplus 3 \ominus 4 \otimes (-2)) = 2(2) + 3 \cdot 4 - (-(-2))" />
  <BlockMath math="= 4 + 12 - (2)" />
  <BlockMath math="= 16 - 2" />
  <BlockMath math="= 14" />
</MathContainer>

Therefore, the value of the operation is <InlineMath math="14" />.
