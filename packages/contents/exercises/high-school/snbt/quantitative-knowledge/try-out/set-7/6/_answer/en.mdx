Given the matrix <InlineMath math="A = \begin{pmatrix} 2 & x-2 \\ -1 & 1 \end{pmatrix}" />. We need to find the value of <InlineMath math="x" /> if <InlineMath math="\det(A) = 8" />.

The determinant of a <InlineMath math="2 \times 2" /> matrix is calculated using the formula <InlineMath math="ad - bc" />.

<MathContainer>
  <BlockMath math="\det(A) = (2)(1) - (x-2)(-1)" />
</MathContainer>

We know that <InlineMath math="\det(A) = 8" />, so:

<MathContainer>
  <BlockMath math="\begin{align*} 8 &= 2 - (-(x-2)) \\ 8 &= 2 + (x-2) \\ 8 &= 2 + x - 2 \\ 8 &= x \end{align*}" />
</MathContainer>

Thus, the value of <InlineMath math="x" /> is <InlineMath math="8" />.
