Kita cek kebenaran setiap pernyataan dengan mengubah pecahan biasa menjadi pecahan desimal.

**Pernyataan:** <InlineMath math="\frac{3}{7} > \frac{1}{3}" />

<MathContainer>
  <BlockMath math="\frac{3}{7} \approx 0,43" />
  <BlockMath math="\frac{1}{3} \approx 0,33" />
  <BlockMath math="0,43 > 0,33 \quad (\text{Benar})" />
</MathContainer>

**Pernyataan:** <InlineMath math="\frac{1}{5} > \frac{2}{9}" />

<MathContainer>
  <BlockMath math="\frac{1}{5} = 0,20" />
  <BlockMath math="\frac{2}{9} \approx 0,22" />
  <BlockMath math="0,20 > 0,22 \quad (\text{Salah})" />
</MathContainer>

**Pernyataan:** <InlineMath math="\frac{2}{3} > \frac{7}{8}" />

<MathContainer>
  <BlockMath math="\frac{2}{3} \approx 0,67" />
  <BlockMath math="\frac{7}{8} = 0,875" />
  <BlockMath math="0,67 > 0,875 \quad (\text{Salah})" />
</MathContainer>

**Pernyataan:** <InlineMath math="\frac{3}{5} > \frac{5}{6}" />

<MathContainer>
  <BlockMath math="\frac{3}{5} = 0,60" />
  <BlockMath math="\frac{5}{6} \approx 0,83" />
  <BlockMath math="0,60 > 0,83 \quad (\text{Salah})" />
</MathContainer>

**Pernyataan:** <InlineMath math="\frac{5}{9} > \frac{3}{4}" />

<MathContainer>
  <BlockMath math="\frac{5}{9} \approx 0,55" />
  <BlockMath math="\frac{3}{4} = 0,75" />
  <BlockMath math="0,55 > 0,75 \quad (\text{Salah})" />
</MathContainer>

Jadi, pernyataan yang benar adalah <InlineMath math="\frac{3}{7} > \frac{1}{3}" />.
