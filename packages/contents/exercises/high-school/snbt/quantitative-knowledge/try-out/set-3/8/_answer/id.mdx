Diketahui <InlineMath math="x = \frac{5}{6}" /> dan <InlineMath math="y = \frac{3}{7}" />.

**Menghitung nilai <InlineMath math="P" />**

<MathContainer>
  <BlockMath math="P = \frac{\frac{1}{x}}{1 + \frac{2}{y}}" />
  <BlockMath math="= \frac{\frac{1}{5/6}}{1 + \frac{2}{3/7}}" />
  <BlockMath math="= \frac{\frac{6}{5}}{1 + 2 \cdot \frac{7}{3}}" />
  <BlockMath math="= \frac{\frac{6}{5}}{1 + \frac{14}{3}}" />
  <BlockMath math="= \frac{\frac{6}{5}}{\frac{3}{3} + \frac{14}{3}}" />
  <BlockMath math="= \frac{\frac{6}{5}}{\frac{17}{3}}" />
  <BlockMath math="= \frac{6}{5} \cdot \frac{3}{17}" />
  <BlockMath math="= \frac{18}{85}" />
</MathContainer>

Nilai <InlineMath math="P = \frac{18}{85}" /> (kurang dari <InlineMath math="1" />, sekitar <InlineMath math="0,21" />).

**Menghitung nilai <InlineMath math="Q" />**

<MathContainer>
  <BlockMath math="Q = \frac{1}{x + y}" />
  <BlockMath math="= \frac{1}{\frac{5}{6} + \frac{3}{7}}" />
  <BlockMath math="= \frac{1}{\frac{35}{42} + \frac{18}{42}}" />
  <BlockMath math="= \frac{1}{\frac{53}{42}}" />
  <BlockMath math="= \frac{42}{53}" />
</MathContainer>

Nilai <InlineMath math="Q = \frac{42}{53}" /> (kurang dari <InlineMath math="1" />, sekitar <InlineMath math="0,79" />).

**Membandingkan <InlineMath math="P" /> dan <InlineMath math="Q" />**

<MathContainer>
  <BlockMath math="P = \frac{18}{85} \approx 0,21" />
  <BlockMath math="Q = \frac{42}{53} \approx 0,79" />
</MathContainer>

Terlihat jelas bahwa <InlineMath math="0,21 < 0,79" />.

Jadi, <InlineMath math="P < Q" />.
