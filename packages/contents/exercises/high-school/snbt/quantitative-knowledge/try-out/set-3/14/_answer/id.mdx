Diketahui matriks <InlineMath math="\mathbf{P} = \begin{pmatrix} 3 - x & -5 \\ 6 & 3 \end{pmatrix}" />.

Rumus determinan matriks <InlineMath math="2 \times 2" />:

<BlockMath math="\det(\mathbf{P}) = \begin{vmatrix} a & b \\ c & d \end{vmatrix} = ad - bc" />

Untuk matriks <InlineMath math="\mathbf{P}" />:

<MathContainer>
  <BlockMath math="\det(\mathbf{P}) = (3 - x)(3) - (-5)(6)" />
  <BlockMath math="= 3(3 - x) - (-30)" />
  <BlockMath math="= 9 - 3x + 30" />
  <BlockMath math="= 39 - 3x" />
</MathContainer>

Diketahui <InlineMath math="\det(\mathbf{P}) = 33" />, maka:

<MathContainer>
  <BlockMath math="39 - 3x = 33" />
  <BlockMath math="-3x = 33 - 39" />
  <BlockMath math="-3x = -6" />
  <BlockMath math="x = \frac{-6}{-3}" />
  <BlockMath math="x = 2" />
</MathContainer>

Jadi, nilai <InlineMath math="x" /> adalah <InlineMath math="2" />.
