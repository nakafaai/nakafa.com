To find the combined average of all classes, we need to calculate the total score of all students and divide it by the total number of students.

**Step 1: Calculating Total Scores for Each Class**

Class IPA (<InlineMath math="n_1 = 30" />, <InlineMath math="\bar{x}_1 = 6.5" />):

<MathContainer>
  <BlockMath math="\Sigma x_1 = n_1 \cdot \bar{x}_1 = 30 \times 6.5 = 195" />
</MathContainer>

Class IPS (<InlineMath math="n_2 = 25" />, <InlineMath math="\bar{x}_2 = 7" />):

<MathContainer>
  <BlockMath math="\Sigma x_2 = n_2 \cdot \bar{x}_2 = 25 \times 7 = 175" />
</MathContainer>

Class Bahasa (<InlineMath math="n_3 = 20" />, <InlineMath math="\bar{x}_3 = 8" />):

<MathContainer>
  <BlockMath math="\Sigma x_3 = n_3 \cdot \bar{x}_3 = 20 \times 8 = 160" />
</MathContainer>

**Step 2: Calculating Combined Average**

The total score of all classes is:

<MathContainer>
  <BlockMath math="\Sigma x_{total} = 195 + 175 + 160 = 530" />
</MathContainer>

The total number of students in all classes is:

<MathContainer>
  <BlockMath math="n_{total} = 30 + 25 + 20 = 75" />
</MathContainer>

Thus, the combined average (<InlineMath math="\bar{x}_{total}" />) is:

<MathContainer>
  <BlockMath math="\bar{x}_{total} = \frac{\Sigma x_{total}}{n_{total}}" />
  <BlockMath math="\bar{x}_{total} = \frac{530}{75}" />
  <BlockMath math="\bar{x}_{total} \approx 7.066..." />
  <BlockMath math="\bar{x}_{total} \approx 7.07" />
</MathContainer>

So, the average score of all these class 12 students is <InlineMath math="7.07" />.
