#### Identify Information

Given:
- Total inventory items <InlineMath math="n(S) = 80" />
- Damaged items <InlineMath math="= 20" />

Thus, the number of undamaged items <InlineMath math="n(A)" /> is:

<MathContainer>
  <BlockMath math="n(A) = 80 - 20" />
  <BlockMath math="n(A) = 60" />
</MathContainer>

#### Probability Calculation

The probability of picking an undamaged item is:

<MathContainer>
  <BlockMath math="P(A) = \frac{n(A)}{n(S)}" />
  <BlockMath math="P(A) = \frac{60}{80}" />
  <BlockMath math="P(A) = \frac{3}{4}" />
</MathContainer>

So, the probability that the item taken is not damaged is <InlineMath math="\frac{3}{4}" />.
