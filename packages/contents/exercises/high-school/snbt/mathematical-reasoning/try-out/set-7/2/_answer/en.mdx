#### Calculating Loan Interest

Given:

- Principal loan (<InlineMath math="M" />) = <InlineMath math="\text{Rp}3,000,000.00" />
- Monthly interest (<InlineMath math="i" />) = <InlineMath math="2\%" />
- Loan duration (<InlineMath math="n" />) = <InlineMath math="10" /> months

The total interest amount is:

<MathContainer>
  <BlockMath math="\text{Interest} = M \times i \times n" />
  <BlockMath math="\text{Interest} = 3,000,000 \times 2\% \times 10" />
  <BlockMath math="\text{Interest} = 3,000,000 \times \frac{2}{100} \times 10" />
  <BlockMath math="\text{Interest} = 600,000" />
</MathContainer>

#### Calculating Monthly Installment

The total amount to be paid is the principal plus total interest:

<MathContainer>
  <BlockMath math="\text{Total} = 3,000,000 + 600,000" />
  <BlockMath math="\text{Total} = 3,600,000" />
</MathContainer>

Since the repayment period is <InlineMath math="10" /> months, the monthly installment is:

<MathContainer>
  <BlockMath math="\text{Installment} = \frac{\text{Total}}{n}" />
  <BlockMath math="\text{Installment} = \frac{3,600,000}{10}" />
  <BlockMath math="\text{Installment} = 360,000" />
</MathContainer>

So, the money Panjul must deposit per month is <InlineMath math="\text{Rp}360,000.00" />.
