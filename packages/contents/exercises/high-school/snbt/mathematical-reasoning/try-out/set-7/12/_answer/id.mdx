#### Menghitung Jarak Sebenarnya

Skala peta adalah <InlineMath math="1 : 2.000.000" />. Jarak sebenarnya (<InlineMath math="J_s" />) dapat dihitung dengan rumus:

<BlockMath math="J_s = J_p \times \text{Skala}" />

**Jarak Sebenarnya Kota <InlineMath math="A" /> ke Kota <InlineMath math="x" />:**

<MathContainer>
  <BlockMath math="J_s(A \to x) = 2,5 \text{ cm} \times 2.000.000" />
  <BlockMath math="= 5.000.000 \text{ cm}" />
  <BlockMath math="= 50 \text{ km}" />
</MathContainer>

**Jarak Sebenarnya Kota <InlineMath math="x" /> ke Kota <InlineMath math="B" />:**

<MathContainer>
  <BlockMath math="J_s(x \to B) = 3 \text{ cm} \times 2.000.000" />
  <BlockMath math="= 6.000.000 \text{ cm}" />
  <BlockMath math="= 60 \text{ km}" />
</MathContainer>

**Jarak Sebenarnya Kota <InlineMath math="A" /> ke Kota <InlineMath math="y" />:**

<MathContainer>
  <BlockMath math="J_s(A \to y) = 4 \text{ cm} \times 2.000.000" />
  <BlockMath math="= 8.000.000 \text{ cm}" />
  <BlockMath math="= 80 \text{ km}" />
</MathContainer>

**Jarak Sebenarnya Kota <InlineMath math="y" /> ke Kota <InlineMath math="B" />:**

<MathContainer>
  <BlockMath math="J_s(y \to B) = 4,5 \text{ cm} \times 2.000.000" />
  <BlockMath math="= 9.000.000 \text{ cm}" />
  <BlockMath math="= 90 \text{ km}" />
</MathContainer>

#### Menghitung Jarak Tempuh

**Perjalanan Benu (<InlineMath math="A \to x \to B" />):**

<MathContainer>
  <BlockMath math="J_{\text{Benu}} = J_s(A \to x) + J_s(x \to B)" />
  <BlockMath math="= 50 \text{ km} + 60 \text{ km}" />
  <BlockMath math="= 110 \text{ km}" />
</MathContainer>

**Perjalanan Mamang (<InlineMath math="A \to y \to B" />):**

<MathContainer>
  <BlockMath math="J_{\text{Mamang}} = J_s(A \to y) + J_s(y \to B)" />
  <BlockMath math="= 80 \text{ km} + 90 \text{ km}" />
  <BlockMath math="= 170 \text{ km}" />
</MathContainer>

#### Menghitung Selisih Jarak

Selisih jarak tempuh Benu dan Mamang adalah:

<MathContainer>
  <BlockMath math="\text{Selisih} = |170 \text{ km} - 110 \text{ km}|" />
  <BlockMath math="= 60 \text{ km}" />
</MathContainer>

Jadi, selisih jarak tempuhnya adalah <InlineMath math="60 \text{ km}" />.
