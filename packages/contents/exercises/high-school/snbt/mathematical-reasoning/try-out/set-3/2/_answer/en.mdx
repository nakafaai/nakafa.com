**Calculating Net Weight (Netto)**

We need to determine the actual amount of rice available for sale. Netto is the gross weight minus the packaging weight (tare).

<MathContainer>
  <BlockMath math="\text{Total Bruto} = 5 \times 50 = 250 \text{ kg}" />
  <BlockMath math="\text{Total Tare} = 1\% \times 250 = 2.5 \text{ kg}" />
  <BlockMath math="\text{Total Netto} = 250 - 2.5 = 247.5 \text{ kg}" />
</MathContainer>

**Calculating Loss**

The merchant incurs a loss because the total selling price is lower than the purchase price.

<MathContainer>
  <BlockMath math="\text{Selling Price} = 247.5 \times 2,000 = \text{Rp}495,000.00" />
  <BlockMath math="\text{Loss} = \text{Purchase Price} - \text{Selling Price}" />
  <BlockMath math="\text{Loss} = 650,000 - 495,000 = \text{Rp}155,000.00" />
</MathContainer>

Therefore, the merchant incurs a loss of <InlineMath math="\text{Rp}155,000.00" />.
