Triangle <InlineMath math="ABC" /> is right-angled at <InlineMath math="A" />, so <InlineMath math="BC" /> is the hypotenuse. The included angle at <InlineMath math="C" /> is <InlineMath math="60^\circ" />, so the sine ratio gives <InlineMath math="AB" /> directly.

<MathContainer>
  <BlockMath math="\sin 60^\circ = \frac{AB}{BC}" />
  <BlockMath math="\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2} = \frac{AB}{500}" />
  <BlockMath math="AB = 500 \cdot \frac{\sqrt{3}}{2} = 250\sqrt{3}" />
</MathContainer>

Thus, the distance from ship <InlineMath math="A" /> to ship <InlineMath math="B" /> is <InlineMath math="250\sqrt{3}\text{ meter}" />.
