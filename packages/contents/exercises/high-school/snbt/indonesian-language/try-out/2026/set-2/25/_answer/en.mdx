The question asks for the predicted cause of death for the bodies that are more than a thousand years old.

This information can be found by connecting sentence <InlineMath math="(3)" /> and <InlineMath math="(12)" />.

Sentence <InlineMath math="(3)" /> mentions a local story about an ancient party trapped in a **mountain hail storm** (*badai es gunung*):

> "Cerita lokal yang berkembang di wilayah pegunungan di India itu mengacu pada sebuah pesta kuno yang terjebak dalam badai es gunung."

Then, sentence <InlineMath math="(12)" /> clarifies the chronology:

> "...alih-alih hanya satu badai yang membunuh mereka semua, penanggalan radiokarbon menunjukkan bahwa penduduk setempat meninggal lebih dari 1.000 tahun yang lalu..."

The phrase "alih-alih hanya satu badai yang membunuh mereka semua" (*instead of just one storm killing them all*) implies that the storm was indeed the cause of death for the first group (the locals who are >1,000 years old), even though it wasn't the cause for the visitor group who arrived much later.

Therefore, the bodies that are more than a thousand years old are predicted to have died due to **Badai** (Storm).
