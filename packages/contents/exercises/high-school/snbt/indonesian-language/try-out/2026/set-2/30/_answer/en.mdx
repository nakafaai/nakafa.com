This question asks for the meaning of the word *strategis* (strategic) in the context of sentence <InlineMath math="(2)" />.

To interpret a word in a sentence, we must consider its context. Sentence <InlineMath math="(2)" /> reads:

> "<InlineMath math="(2)" />Lokasinya yang **strategis** memudahkan banyak orang dari berbagai tempat untuk datang."
> (*Its **strategic** location makes it easy for many people from various places to come.*)

The word *strategis* here describes "location". Something strategic in the context of location usually means the place is easily accessible, advantageous, or well-positioned.

According to the Kamus Besar Bahasa Indonesia (KBBI), *strategis* can mean related to strategy or **baik letaknya** (good location/position).

Thus, in this context, *strategis* means that its location or position is good, making it easy for people to visit.

Therefore, the most appropriate answer is **Baik letaknya** (Well-positioned).
