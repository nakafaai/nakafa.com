The missing part (*bagian rumpang*) appears right before the sentence:

> *"Cuma fase saya saat itu datang lebih awal..."* (*Only my phase came earlier at that time...*)

The word **"Cuma"** (*Only/But*) indicates a contrast or exception to the previous idea. The author implies that the experience itself isn't unique, but the **timing** is.

**Logical Flow:**

If the previous sentence suggests that **everyone might experience this too**, then the connection becomes clear:

*"Everyone might experience this phase, **only** mine happened earlier."*

Therefore, the most appropriate sentence to complete the paragraph is:

> **"Mungkin setiap orang akan mengalami fase ini juga"** (*Maybe everyone will experience this phase too*)

This choice creates a coherent and logical flow with the subsequent sentence.
