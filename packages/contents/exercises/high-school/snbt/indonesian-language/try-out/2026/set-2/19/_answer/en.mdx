The question asks us to identify the atmosphere or condition describing the "prison" in the bakery based on the text.

This information can be found directly in sentence <InlineMath math="(8)" />:

> "Menurut pernyataan dari Archaeological Park of Pompeii, **ruangan sempit itu tidak memiliki pemandangan dunia luar**."

This sentence clearly describes two main characteristics of the room:
1.  Narrow (*ruangan sempit*).
2.  Closed off from the outside world (*tidak memiliki pemandangan dunia luar*).

Other options such as "spacious and humid" (*luas dan lembab*), "tight protection" (*perlindungan ketat*), "iron room" (*ruangan besi*), or "underground" (*bawah tanah*) do not match the specific description given in that sentence.

Therefore, the most appropriate answer is **Ruangan sempit dan tidak ada pandangan dunia** (A narrow room with no view of the outside world).
