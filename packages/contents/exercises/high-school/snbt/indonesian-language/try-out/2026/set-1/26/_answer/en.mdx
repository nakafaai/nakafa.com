To determine the atmosphere or setting of a paragraph, we need to observe the descriptive details presented by the author. Let's dissect the first paragraph:

1.  **Specific Location**: Explicitly mentioned "Di kampung pesisir Werur" (In the coastal village of Werur).
2.  **Natural Scenery**: Descriptions of "birunya laut begitu terhampar luas" (the blue sea spread so wide) and "ombaknya bagai menampar pesisir" (the waves slapping the coast).
3.  **Human Activity**: The construction of a sea wall by BNPB and children playing near it.
4.  **Environmental Issue**: Mentions of abrasion consuming the coastal area.

All these elements—sea, waves, beach sand, abrasion walls, and community life (children playing)—collectively paint a portrait of life in a **"perkampungan yang terletak di pesisir pantai"** (village located on the coast).

Other options like "Desa wisata" (Tourism village) or "Proyek pemerintah" (Government project) only capture a small part of the whole picture, whereas "perkampungan yang terletak di pesisir pantai" encapsulates the entire atmosphere built in the paragraph comprehensively.

Therefore, the atmosphere described is a village located on the coast.
