**Latar tempat** (setting of place) refers to the location where the events in the story or text occur.

Based on the text, the main setting where all events and descriptions are centered is **Kampung Pesisir Werur**.

This information is explicitly stated in the very first sentence <InlineMath math="(1)" />: *"Di kampung pesisir Werur, birunya laut begitu terhampar luas..."* (In the coastal village of Werur, the blue sea stretches as far as the eye can see...).

All subsequent descriptions—from the waves, the embankment construction by BNPB Tambrauw, to the historical background involving the Allies and Japanese Empire—revolve around this specific location.

Therefore, the most appropriate answer is **Kampung Pesisir Werur**.
