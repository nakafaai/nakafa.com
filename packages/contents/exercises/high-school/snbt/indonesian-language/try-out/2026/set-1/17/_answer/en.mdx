The question asks for the reason why Norma Jeane was put into an orphanage in 1935.

This information can be found in the second paragraph of the text. In sentence <InlineMath math="(9)" />, it is mentioned that Gladys (Norma Jeane's mother) suffered from mental illness in January 1934 and was diagnosed with paranoid schizophrenia.

As a result, she was institutionalized in a mental health facility <InlineMath math="(10)" />. Due to her absence, Norma Jeane was handled by the state <InlineMath math="(11)" /> and then put into an orphanage in 1935 <InlineMath math="(12)" />.

Based on this information, the reason Norma Jeane was put into an orphanage was that there was no one to care for her because her mother was suffering from mental illness and had to be treated in a health facility.

Therefore, the correct answer is *tidak ada yang merawat Norma Jeane pada saat itu lantaran ibunya mengalami gangguan mental*.
