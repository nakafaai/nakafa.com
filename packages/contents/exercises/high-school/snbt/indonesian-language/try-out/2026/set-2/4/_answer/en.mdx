The question asks for the reason why rice is referred to as a versatile ingredient (*bahan serbaguna*) in the text.

Pay attention to sentence <InlineMath math="(5)" />: "Beras adalah bahan serbaguna untuk banyak hidangan ikonik dari seluruh dunia, termasuk ketupat dan lontong dari Indonesia, dolmades dari Yunani, risotto dari Italia, paella dari Spanyol, dan puding beras dari Inggris."

The sentence explicitly explains that rice is called versatile because it can be processed into various iconic dishes from different countries around the world. Other answer choices may contain facts that are true according to the text, but they are not the reason why rice is called "versatile" (*serbaguna*).

Therefore, the correct reason is because **Beras dapat dijadikan banyak hidangan ikonik dari seluruh dunia**.
