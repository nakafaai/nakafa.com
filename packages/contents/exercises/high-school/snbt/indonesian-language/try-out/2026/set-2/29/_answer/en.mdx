The question asks us to predict the topic of the next paragraph based on the end of the text.

The topic of a new paragraph usually relates closely to the last sentence of the previous paragraph. Let's look at sentence <InlineMath math="(13)" />:

> "<InlineMath math="(13)" />Lebih lanjut, Agni menjelaskan bahwa **pemilik arsip tersebut merupakan orang yang sangat berjasa dalam melestarikan arsip sejarah Lasem**."
> (*Furthermore, Agni explained that the **owner of the archive is a person who has made a significant contribution to preserving Lasem's historical archives**.*)

This sentence introduces a new specific subject: the "archive owner" who is called "very meritorious". However, the text has not yet explained who this person is or what their specific contributions are.

Therefore, the next paragraph will most likely discuss in more detail about **the figure who preserves Lasem batik** (the archive owner).

Thus, the most appropriate answer is **sosok yang melestraikan batik Lasem**.
