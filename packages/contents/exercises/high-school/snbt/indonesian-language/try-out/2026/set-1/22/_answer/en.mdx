The question asks for the lexical meaning of the word *kompromi* in sentence <InlineMath math="(9)" />.

Lexical meaning refers to the meaning found in the dictionary, specifically the *Kamus Besar Bahasa Indonesia* (KBBI). According to the KBBI, the word *kompromi* means "persetujuan dengan jalan damai" (agreement via peaceful means).

Therefore, the most appropriate answer is **Persetujuan dengan jalan damai**.
