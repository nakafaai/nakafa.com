This question asks for the reason why Indonesia is called the main source of plastic waste based on the reading text.

The answer can be found explicitly in the first sentence <InlineMath math="(1)" />: *"Indonesia disebut-sebut sebagai negara sumber utama sampah plastik berbasis darat yang ditemukan di pantai-pantai Seychelles, sebuah negara kepulauan di Afrika"* (Indonesia is cited as the main source country of land-based plastic waste found on the beaches of Seychelles, an archipelago nation in Africa).

Subsequent sentences in the text also support this statement by explaining studies that found and predicted the accumulation of plastic waste from Indonesia in that location. Therefore, the main reason is due to the discovery of plastic waste from Indonesia on the beaches of Seychelles.
