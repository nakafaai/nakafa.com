The question asks for the strange/odd thing found in Roopkund Lake.

This is explicitly explained in sentence <InlineMath math="(4)" />:

> "Namun, analisis terhadap usia dan genom tulang mengungkapkan sesuatu yang jauh lebih aneh dan sulit dijelaskan: **para korban terpisah 1.000 tahun dan memiliki asal-usul yang sangat berbeda**."

This sentence indicates the strangeness of the discovery, which is the vast difference in time of death (1,000 years) and diverse origins, refuting the initial assumption that they died simultaneously in a single event.

Therefore, the correct answer is **Para korban yang ditemukan di danau Roopkund berdasarkan analisa terpaut jauh dan asal usul berbeda** (The victims found in Roopkund Lake based on analysis are far apart in time and have different origins).
