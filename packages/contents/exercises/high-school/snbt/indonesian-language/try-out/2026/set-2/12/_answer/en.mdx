This question asks about the reason for selecting female elephants for GPS Collar installation.

Let's refer back to the text, specifically sentence <InlineMath math="(22)" />:

> "Utamanya adalah gajah betina dewasa yang menjadi pemimpin klan." (Mainly adult females who are clan leaders.)

This sentence explicitly states that female elephants are chosen, especially adults, because of their role as group or clan leaders. This is crucial because the clan leader will be followed by the other members, so by tracking one leader, the movement of the entire group can be monitored.

Therefore, the primary reason for installing GPS on female elephants is their position as group leaders.

So, the correct answer is **Gajah betina merupakan pemimpin kelompok gajah**.
