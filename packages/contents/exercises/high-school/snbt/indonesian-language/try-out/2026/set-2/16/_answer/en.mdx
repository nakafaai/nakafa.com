The question asks for the *hal yang menguntungkan masa kini* (benefit for the present day) resulting from the discoveries in Pompeii.

To find the answer, we need to look at the first paragraph, specifically sentence <InlineMath math="(3)" />:

> "Dalam sejarah dunia, penemuan-penemuan di Pompeii itu membantu kita **memahami tentang kehidupan masyarakat di masa itu**."

This sentence clearly states the main benefit: these discoveries help us *memahami* (understand) the life of the society at that time. While the text mentions specific findings like *kereta seremonial* (ceremonial chariot) or *kios makanan* (food kiosk), the broader advantage is the insight they provide into ancient life.

Therefore, the correct answer is **Dapat memahami tentang kehidupan masyarakat di masa itu** (Can understand about the life of the people at that time).
