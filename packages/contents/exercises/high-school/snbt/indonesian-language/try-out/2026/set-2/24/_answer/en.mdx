The question asks about the majority ethnicity found by researchers in Roopkund Lake.

This information is explicitly found in sentence <InlineMath math="(8)" />:

> "Seperti yang diperkirakan, **mayoritas mayat di danau itu adalah orang India**."

The sentence clearly states that the majority of the skeletons found are of Indian ethnicity.

Therefore, the correct answer is **India**.
