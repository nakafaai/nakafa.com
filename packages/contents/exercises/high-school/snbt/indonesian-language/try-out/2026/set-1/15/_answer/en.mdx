This question asks for the **lexical meaning** (makna leksikal) of the word "senja" from the text.

Lexical meaning refers to the dictionary definition of a word, independent of its specific usage in a sentence. According to the **Kamus Besar Bahasa Indonesia (KBBI)**, the word "senja" is defined as *waktu (hari) setengah gelap sesudah matahari terbenam* (the time of semi-darkness after the sun sets).

Based on this definition, the correct answer among the choices is *suasana saat matahari tenggelam*.

Therefore, the lexical meaning of "senja" is *suasana saat matahari tenggelam*.
