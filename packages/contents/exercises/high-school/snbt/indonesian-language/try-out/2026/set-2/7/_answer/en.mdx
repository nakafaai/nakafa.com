One of the advantages of the Pickatale application is that its reading materials are available in various languages.

This information can be found in the text in sentence <InlineMath math="(5)" />, which states: "Selain jenis bacaan yang bermacam-macam, di sini kita juga bisa menikmati bacaan dari berbagai bahasa, seperti bahasa Inggris, Denmark, Norwegia, dan tentu saja Indonesia."

Therefore, the most appropriate answer is **Bacaan tersedia dalam berbagai bahasa**.
