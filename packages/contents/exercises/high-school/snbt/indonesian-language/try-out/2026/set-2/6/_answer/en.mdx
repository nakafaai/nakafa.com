The question asks about the types of reading material available on the Pickatale app.

This information can be found explicitly in sentence <InlineMath math="(4)" />: "Tak cuma dongeng dan fiksi, di sini juga terdapat bacaan tentang pengetahuan, sejarah, biografi, dan lain sebagainya."

The sentence confirms that besides fairy tales (*dongeng*), the application also provides reading material about general knowledge (*pengetahuan*). This is further supported by sentence <InlineMath math="(19)" />, which states that "selain itu dongeng-dongeng ini juga diselipkan pengetahuan-pengetahuan menarik."

Therefore, the correct answer is **Dongeng dan pengetahuan**.
