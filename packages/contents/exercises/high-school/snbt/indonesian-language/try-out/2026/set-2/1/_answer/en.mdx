The main idea (*gagasan pokok*) is the central concept that drives the development of a text. To find it, we need to trace the flow of discussion from beginning to end.

Although the first paragraph opens with the issue of rice prices and its importance as a staple food, the second paragraph immediately narrows down to the core question: *"apakah Anda harus mencuci beras terlebih dahulu sebelum dimasak"* (whether you should wash rice before cooking it). The third paragraph reinforces this focus by presenting an expert opinion (Evangeline Mantzioris) regarding the effects of washing rice on starch content.

Thus, the entire text actually converges on one main topic discussed in depth, which is **washing rice before cooking** (*mencuci beras sebelum dimasak*). Other options such as rice prices, universal appeal, or Evangeline's profile are merely supporting details or introductions, not the core issue being discussed.

Therefore, the most accurate answer is **Mencuci beras sebelum dimasak**.
