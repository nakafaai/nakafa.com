A paragraph must relate to one another; the information between paragraphs must be connected and should not deviate from the topic raised in the text. Furthermore, there must be continuity between paragraphs. This point must be carefully considered when writing a paragraph.

Based on this, it can be seen that the last paragraph of the text discusses washing rice before cooking, which can reduce the starch in the rice. However, unfortunately, the most important follow-up information regarding **how to cook rice without reducing starch** (*cara memasak nasi tanpa mengurangi pati*) has not yet been conveyed.

Therefore, this needs to be presented to further clarify the reading text, making the most appropriate answer **Informasi lebih lanjut terkait cara memasak nasi tanpa mengurangi pati**.
