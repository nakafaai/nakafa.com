The question asks for the **consequence** (*akibat*) of washing rice before cooking. A consequence is the result or effect caused by an event.

To find the answer, we need to look for specific information regarding the impact of washing rice in the text. Pay attention to sentence <InlineMath math="(10)" />:

*"Para pakar kuliner mengeklaim beras yang dicuci terlebih dahulu dapat mengurangi jumlah pati yang berasal dari butiran beras."* (Culinary experts claim that washing rice beforehand can reduce the amount of starch coming from the rice grains.)

This sentence explicitly states that the result (consequence) of washing rice is the reduction of starch content. Other options are not mentioned in the text as direct consequences of the rice washing process.

Therefore, the most accurate answer is **Jumlah pati yang berasal dari butiran beras berkurang**.
