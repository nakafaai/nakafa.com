The possible impact on the earth if there were no humans for <InlineMath math="50\text{ years}" /> is that the Earth's condition would improve from the damage that has occurred (*Kondisi Bumi akan membaik dari kerusakan yang terjadi*).

This is answered by the statement in sentence <InlineMath math="(10)" /> which emphasizes the clause "...the earth could recover itself" (*...bumi bisa kembali memulihkan dirinya sendiri*). This fragment implies that the earth will improve on its own from the damage caused by human activities.

Other options are incorrect because:
- **Earth will return to ancient conditions** (*Bumi akan kembali kondisi jaman dulu*): No indication of returning to the past.
- **Air pollution will no longer occur** (*Polusi udara tidak akan terjadi lagi*): Only one aspect, while sentence <InlineMath math="(10)" /> covers general recovery.
- **A new better life will be created** (*Kehidupan baru yang lebih baik akan tercipta*): Too speculative and not supported by the text.
- **Earth will be uncontrolled** (*Bumi akan tidak terkendali*): On the contrary, the earth can recover itself.
