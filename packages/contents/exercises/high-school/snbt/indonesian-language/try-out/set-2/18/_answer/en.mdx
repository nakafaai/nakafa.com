The question asks for the reason behind the existence of a "prison" inside the bakery based on the text.

The answer can be found explicitly in sentence <InlineMath math="(7)" />:

> "Di tempat itu, **manusia dan hewan tinggal dan bekerja keras** dalam kondisi brutal **untuk membuat roti**."

This sentence explains that the function of the place referred to as a "prison" was to house and force labor upon humans (slaves) and animals (such as the donkeys mentioned in sentence <InlineMath math="(10)" />) for the bread production process.

Other options such as a secret prison (*penjara rahasia*), colony headquarters (*markas koloni*), weapons vault (*brankas senjata*), or a place for murder (*tempat pembunuhan*) are not supported by the text.

Therefore, the reason for the prison in the bakery is that **manusia dan hewan diperbudak untuk membuat roti** (humans and animals were enslaved to make bread).
