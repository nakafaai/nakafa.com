The activity performed by Uwet in the story *Jalan di Atas Duri* by Vanda Parengkuan is that Uwet is picking cherries.

This information can be found in the reading text from sentence <InlineMath math="(7)" /> to sentence <InlineMath math="(11)" />. Sentence <InlineMath math="(7)" /> states: "Dongeng ini menceritakan tentang liliput bernama Uwet yang ingin mengambil buah ceri."

Therefore, the most appropriate answer is **Uwet mengambil buah ceri**.
