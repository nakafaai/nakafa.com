The question asks what is most highlighted in the recent discovery in Pompeii based on the text.

This answer is stated explicitly in sentence <InlineMath math="(4)" />:

> "Sebuah penemuan terbaru di Pompeii menyoroti bagian yang sering diabaikan dalam masyarakat: **perbudakan**."

This sentence confirms that the main focus of the recent discovery is regarding slavery (*perbudakan*). Although the text mentions other discoveries such as ceremonial chariots, food stalls, and frescoes in sentence <InlineMath math="(2)" />, those are mentioned as discoveries "in recent years" generally, not the specific focus of the "latest discovery" discussed in depth in the subsequent paragraphs.

Therefore, the correct answer is **Perbudakan** (Slavery).
