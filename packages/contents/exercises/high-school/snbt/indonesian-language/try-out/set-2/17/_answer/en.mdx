The question asks for the physical evidence found in Pompeii that indicates the existence of slavery practices.

To answer this, we need to refer to specific information in the reading text.

Sentence <InlineMath math="(6)" /> states: "Baru-baru ini, mereka mengumumkan tentang penemuan **“penjara” di sebuah toko roti**" (Recently, they announced the discovery of a "prison" in a bakery).

Sentence <InlineMath math="(7)" /> further explains: "Di tempat itu, manusia dan hewan tinggal dan bekerja keras dalam kondisi brutal untuk membuat roti" (In that place, humans and animals lived and worked hard in brutal conditions to make bread).

This is reinforced in sentence <InlineMath math="(13)" />, which concludes that this finding shows a shocking side of ancient slavery.

Other options such as *tulang benulang* (bones), *alat bunuh diri* (suicide tools), or *gambar para budak* (pictures of slaves) are not specifically mentioned as the main evidence of this recent discovery in the text.

Therefore, the main evidence found is the existence of a **"penjara" di sebuah toko roti** ("prison" in a bakery).
