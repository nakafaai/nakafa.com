The question asks for the reason why Roopkund Lake is nicknamed "Skeleton Lake" (*Danau Tengkorak*).

This information is found at the beginning of the first paragraph, specifically in sentences <InlineMath math="(1)" /> and <InlineMath math="(2)" />:

> "<InlineMath math="(1)" />Danau Roopkund di Pegunungan Himalaya memiliki **beberapa ratus mayat yang tersebar di sekitar dan di dalamnya**. <InlineMath math="(2)" />**Oleh karena itu**, Danau Roopkund mendapat julukan sebagai “Danau Tengkorak”."

The conjunction "Therefore" (*Oleh karena itu*) in sentence <InlineMath math="(2)" /> directly links the nickname to the fact mentioned in the previous sentence, which is the presence of hundreds of bodies in the lake.

Other options such as suicide, ancient settlements, wars, or natural curses are speculations or local stories not mentioned as the primary reason for the nickname in the text.

Therefore, the correct reason is **Banyak mayat yang ditemukan di sekitar maupun di dalam danau tersebut** (Many bodies were found around and inside the lake).
