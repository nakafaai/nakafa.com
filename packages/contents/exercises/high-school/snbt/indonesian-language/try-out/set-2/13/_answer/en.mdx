This question asks us to determine the lexical meaning of the word *mitigasi* found in sentence <InlineMath math="(17)" />.

Lexical meaning is the meaning of a word as it appears in the dictionary, in this case, the *Kamus Besar Bahasa Indonesia* (KBBI). Let's look at the context of the sentence:

> "...mendorong desain strategi pengelolaan konservasi gajah dan mitigasi konfliknya." (...encouraging the design of elephant conservation management strategies and conflict mitigation.)

In that sentence, *mitigasi* relates to efforts to handle conflicts between elephants and humans. If we refer to the KBBI, the word **mitigasi** means:
1. Taming (*Penjinakan*).
2. Action to reduce the impact of a disaster (*Tindakan mengurangi dampak bencana*).

In the context of the environment and disasters (including wildlife conflicts that cause damage), the most relevant and commonly used definition is the second one, which is actions taken to reduce the negative impact or risk of an event (disaster).

Therefore, the correct answer is **Tindakan mengurangi dampak bencana** (Action to reduce the impact of a disaster).
