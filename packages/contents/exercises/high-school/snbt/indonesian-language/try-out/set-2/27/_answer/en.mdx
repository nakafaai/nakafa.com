The question asks for the lexical meaning of the word *kosmopolitan* (cosmopolitan) which appears in the text.

Lexical meaning is the meaning of a word as found in the dictionary (KBBI). According to the Great Dictionary of the Indonesian Language (KBBI), the word *kosmopolitan* means:

> "Mempunyai wawasan dan pengetahuan yang luas; terjadi dari orang-orang atau unsur-unsur yang berasal dari berbagai penjuru dunia."
> (*Having broad insight and knowledge; consisting of people or elements from various parts of the world.*)

In the context of sentence <InlineMath math="(1)" />, "Sejak lama, Lasem menjadi kawasan kosmopolitan...", this word refers to the nature of Lasem which is open and has broad insight due to interactions with various cultures.

Therefore, the most appropriate meaning according to the answer choices is **wawasan dan pengetahuan luas** (broad insight and knowledge).
