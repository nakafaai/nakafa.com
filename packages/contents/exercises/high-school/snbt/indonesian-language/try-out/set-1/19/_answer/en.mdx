The question asks for an exemplary attitude from Norma Jeane.

The attitude that can be exemplified from the character can be found in sentence <InlineMath math="(20)" />. It is stated that: "Norma Jeane kerap menulis dengan tekun untuk surat kabar sekolah" (Norma Jeane often wrote diligently for the school newspaper).

The keyword "tekun" in that sentence indicates that Norma Jeane had the trait of diligence (*ketekunan*) in doing something she was interested in, even though she was not an academically high-achieving student (sentence <InlineMath math="(19)" />).

Therefore, the correct answer is **Sosok yang tekun**.
