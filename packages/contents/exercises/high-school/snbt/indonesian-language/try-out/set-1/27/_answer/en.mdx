To identify who Fera Ramar is, we need to trace the sentences that refer directly to her in the text.

Observe sentence <InlineMath math="(6)" />: *"Dulunya laut tidak sedekat ini, masih jauh lagi di depan sana," kata Fera Ramar...*

Here, Fera Ramar is introduced as the source. Her professional identity is then explicitly confirmed in the following sentence, sentence <InlineMath math="(7)" />: *"Dia adalah seorang guru di SD YPK Imanuel Werur."*

This information is very clear and unambiguous. Although Fera was met at a traditional festival (sentence 8), her main profession mentioned in the text is as an educator or teacher.

Therefore, the figure of Fera in the text is a **Guru** (Teacher).
