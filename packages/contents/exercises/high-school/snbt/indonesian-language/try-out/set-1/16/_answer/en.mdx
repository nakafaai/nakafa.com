The question asks for the reason why Marilyn Monroe is remembered to this day.

This information can be found in sentence <InlineMath math="(3)" /> of the text: *"Monroe adalah seorang aktris panggung utama selama satu dekade penuh."* (Monroe was a leading stage actress for a full decade).

Based on this sentence, what makes Marilyn Monroe remembered is that she was a leading stage actress (*aktris panggung utama*) of her time.

Therefore, the correct answer is *aktris panggung utama*.
