Character traits can be identified through a person's actions. In the first paragraph, it is stated that Fei Febri made a "crucial decision" (*keputusan krusial*) to "switch careers" (*banting setir karier*).

She left her established position as a *corporate support division head* at a multinational company to dive into local waste management. The act of leaving a comfort zone and a high-ranking position for something new and challenging shows that Fei is a person who is **willing to take risks** (*berani mengambil risiko*).

Other options are less appropriate because:
- **Prioritizing work comfort** (*Mengutamakan kenyamanan kerja*): Contradicts her action of leaving an established position.
- **Very thorough and tenacious** (*Sangat teliti dan ulet*): While she may possess these traits, the first paragraph focuses on her bold career move.
- **A neat person** (*Sosok yang rapih*): Not specifically mentioned.
- **Cares about humanity** (*Peduli terhadap kemanusiaan*): Although her new field has social impact, the primary focus of the first paragraph is on the boldness of her career decision.
