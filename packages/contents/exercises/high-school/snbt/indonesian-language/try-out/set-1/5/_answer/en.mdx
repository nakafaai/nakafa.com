Background (*latar belakang*) refers to the fundamental reason driving an action. In the text, the reason behind Fei Febri's decision to enter waste management is explicitly stated in sentences <InlineMath math="(15)" /> and <InlineMath math="(16)" />.

Fei Febri expresses her personal restlessness regarding waste because she produces it every day (*"...kegelisahan saya sendiri itu adalah dari sisi sampah. Kenapa? Karena saya menghasilkan sampah setiap hari"*).

It is this **awareness of the waste she produces herself** (*kesadaran akan sampah yang ia hasilkan sendiri*) that serves as the primary trigger, rather than just general environmental issues or national waste management conditions.

Therefore, the most appropriate answer is **kesadaran akan sampah yang ia hasilkan sendiri**.
