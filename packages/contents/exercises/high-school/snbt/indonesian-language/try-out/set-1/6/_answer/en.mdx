Something that strengthens information is usually the presence of facts that can prove the truth of that information.

In the text, the information regarding Indonesia as a contributor of plastic waste to Africa (Seychelles) is proven by research results. This is explicitly mentioned in sentence <InlineMath math="(2)" />:

> "Hal itu dikatakan dalam makalah studi yang terbit di jurnal internasional Marine Debris Pollution..." (*That was stated in a study paper published in the international journal Marine Debris Pollution...*)

Scientific research in a credible journal like *Marine Debris Pollution* serves as strong evidence (fact) that validates the statement. The other options might be relevant as contributing factors, but the primary supporting evidence is the study result itself.

Therefore, the answer is **hasil penelitian dari jurnal yang diterbitkan di Jurnal Marine Debris Pollution**.
