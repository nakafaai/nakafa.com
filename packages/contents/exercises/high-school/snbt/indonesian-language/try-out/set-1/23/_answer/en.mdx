The thing that is often overlooked when deciding to go to war is the impact of the war itself. Very often, war is waged due to overflowing emotions and revenge without thinking about the future impact that affects all aspects.

This is in accordance with the information in the reading text in sentence <InlineMath math="(17)" />, namely "Dan meskipun ada alasan di balik setiap perang, dan setiap perang memiliki alasan, ada banyak cara logis yang bisa dilakukan masyarakat untuk mengabaikan dampak perang—ada lima, tepatnya."

So, the correct answer is **Dampak perang**.
