The question asks for the **denotative meaning** of the word *"petuah"* in sentence <InlineMath math="(2)" />.

**Denotative meaning** is the literal, dictionary definition of a word, as opposed to its connotative (figurative) meaning.

The word **"petuah"** in Indonesian means advice, counsel, or instruction given by a wise or religious person. It is synonymous with **"nasihat"** (advice).

Therefore, the correct denotative meaning is **Nasihat** (Advice).
