Given map scale <InlineMath math="1 : 200,000" />, map distance <InlineMath math="12 \text{ cm}" />, and travel time <InlineMath math="12 \text{ minutes}" />.

Calculate the actual distance using the map scale

<BlockMath math="\frac{\text{Map distance}}{\text{Actual distance}} = \frac{1}{200,000}" />

<BlockMath math="\frac{12 \text{ cm}}{x} = \frac{1}{200,000}" />

<BlockMath math="x = 12 \times 200,000 = 2,400,000 \text{ cm}" />

Convert to kilometers

<BlockMath math="x = 2,400,000 \text{ cm} = 24 \text{ km}" />

Convert time to hours

<BlockMath math="t = 12 \text{ minutes} = \frac{12}{60} \text{ hours} = \frac{1}{5} \text{ hours}" />

Calculate average speed

<MathContainer>
<BlockMath math="v = \frac{x}{t}" />

<BlockMath math="v = \frac{24 \text{ km}}{\frac{1}{5} \text{ hours}}" />

<BlockMath math="v = 24 \times 5 = 120 \text{ km/h}" />
</MathContainer>

Therefore, the average speed of the airplane is <InlineMath math="120 \text{ km/h}" />.
