The word "eliminate" in the sentence "Improved flexibility can help eliminate aches and pains..." means to get rid of or relieve pain. In this context, the word with the closest meaning is "Relieve", which means to alleviate or reduce pain.

Let's analyze the other options:
*   **Increase**: To make greater (antonym of eliminate).
*   **Delete**: To erase (usually used for data or text, not physical pain).
*   **Remove**: To take away (similar, but "relieve" is more idiomatic for pain).
*   **Enhance**: To improve or increase (usually positive qualities).

Therefore, the most appropriate answer is **Relieve**.
