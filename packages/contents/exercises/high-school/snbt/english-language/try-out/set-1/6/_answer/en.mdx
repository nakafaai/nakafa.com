The author assumes that the successful integration of new technology in education requires helpful and good teachers.

The text consistently emphasizes the critical role of educators. For instance, the last paragraph mentions: *"...leaving it to talented educators and caring adults to determine how best to leverage each new technology..."*

Furthermore, the text states that technology integration is successful when it *supports the goals of educators*. This implies an assumption that teachers play a key role in determining the success of technology use in the classroom.
