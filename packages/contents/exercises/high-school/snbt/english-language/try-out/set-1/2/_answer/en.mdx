In the sentence "In Indonesia, Internet *burst* has been mainly used...", the word *burst* refers to the sudden surge or widespread appearance of the internet in society.

The word **presence** is the most appropriate match to describe this massive existence in this context. Other options like *penetration* are less accurate because *burst* here emphasizes the "appearance" or "existence" aspect.

Therefore, the word that best replaces it is **presence**.
