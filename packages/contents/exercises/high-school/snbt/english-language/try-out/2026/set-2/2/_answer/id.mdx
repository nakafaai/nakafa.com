Pertanyaan ini meminta kita mencari pernyataan yang **SALAH** (*FALSE*) berdasarkan teks.

Mari kita analisis pilihan jawaban:

*   **It is said that regular swimming helps older people stay mentally and physically fit**: Benar. Paragraf 1 menyebutkan: *"regular swimming helps older people stay mentally and physically fit"*.
*   **The unique benefits of water make it the perfect place for people of all ages to exercise**: Benar. Paragraf 1 menyebutkan: *"unique benefits of water make it the perfect place for people of all ages to exercise"*.
*   **Swimming can only give benefits for the human mind that can improve our mood**: **Salah**. Teks menyebutkan manfaat fisik yang luas seperti *"problems with their joints and muscles"* dan *"swimmers live longer"*, bukan hanya untuk pikiran/mental saja. Kata "only" membuat pernyataan ini salah.
*   **The findings will further be used to identify other health benefits of swimming**: Benar. Paragraf 2 menyebutkan: *"invest in further research on the impact of physical activity on mental health and long term conditions"*.
*   **The unique properties of water mean that unlike other activities, no one is restricted from taking part**: Benar. Paragraf 2 menyebutkan: *"The unique properties of water mean that unlike other activities, no one is restricted from taking part"*.

Jadi, pernyataan yang salah adalah bahwa berenang hanya memberikan manfaat bagi pikiran manusia saja.
