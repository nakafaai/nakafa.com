The text discusses a study by Lund University comparing the immune systems of sedentary and migratory birds.

The core discussion is how **migration** drives evolutionary changes in the bird's **immune system**. Specifically, migratory birds have evolved less complex immune systems compared to their sedentary African ancestors due to lower disease exposure in Europe.
