The importance of using technology and media wisely to support existing classroom practice is explained in **Paragraph 2** and **Paragraph 3**.

**Paragraph 2** states: *"early childhood classrooms can benefit from the possibilities of extending children’s learning through judicious use of these tools."* The term *"judicious use"* means wise use. This paragraph also explains that technology should support existing educational goals.

**Paragraph 3** continues by explaining how focusing on technology as tools rather than ends in themselves, and emphasizing *intentionality* as a key to developmentally appropriate use.
