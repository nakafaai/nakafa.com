This question asks for a restatement of the findings in paragraph 2.

The original sentences state: *"The authors of the review found statistically significant weight loss in 21 (60%) of the studies. When pooled, the studies in this meta-analysis showed a 1.24 kg weight reduction during the month of Ramadan fasting."*

The core message is that a majority of the studies found statistically significant weight loss during Ramadan.

Analysis of options:

*   **a significant finding during Ramadan fasting is weight reduction**: This is the most accurate restatement. It captures the key point that the significant finding (*statistically significant weight loss*) was weight reduction.
*   **fasting people will be good at body shape**: The text discusses weight loss, not necessarily "good body shape."
*   **being slim should be the aim...**: This expresses an opinion about goals, not the factual findings of the study.
*   **people’s physical fitness is closely related...**: Too broad and misses the specific finding of weight reduction.
*   **weight loss in people means they do the appropriate Ramadan fasting**: Incorrect, the study does not link weight loss to the "appropriateness" of the fasting.
