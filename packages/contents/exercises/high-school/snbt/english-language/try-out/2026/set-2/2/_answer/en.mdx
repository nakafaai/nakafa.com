This question asks us to identify the statement that is **FALSE** according to the text.

Let's analyze the options:

*   **It is said that regular swimming helps older people stay mentally and physically fit**: True. Paragraph 1 states: *"regular swimming helps older people stay mentally and physically fit"*.
*   **The unique benefits of water make it the perfect place for people of all ages to exercise**: True. Paragraph 1 states: *"unique benefits of water make it the perfect place for people of all ages to exercise"*.
