This question asks for a pronoun reference.

Look at the sentence in the first paragraph: *"In a unique study, **researchers** at Lund University in Sweden have mapped the origins of migratory birds. **They** used the results to investigate..."*

The word **"They"** refers to the plural subject in the previous sentence who performed the action of "using the results." That subject is the researchers from Lund University.

Analysis of options:

*   **Researchers**: This option is correct because the researchers are the ones conducting the study and using the results.
*   **The origins**: The object being mapped, not the subject doing the research.
*   **Migratory birds**: The subject of the study, not the investigators.
*   **Immune systems**: The topic being investigated for differences.
*   **Major differences**: The findings, not the actors.
