The idea of "freedom to use varied learning resources" can be found in the third paragraph.

The key sentence is: *"Interactive digital content allows students to pick particular topics that they want to explore more. In a nutshell, there is a democratization of the learning process."*

The word **democratization** here summarizes the concept of students' freedom to choose and explore learning materials as they wish, reflecting equal access and autonomy in learning.

Therefore, the word that best represents this idea is **democratization**.
