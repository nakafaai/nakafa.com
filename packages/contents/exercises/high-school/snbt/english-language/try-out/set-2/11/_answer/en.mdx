The author's attitude is **persuasive**.

The author goes beyond merely stating scientific facts; they actively urge readers to change their lifestyle. This is evident from the frequent use of imperative sentences (commands) such as:

*   *"Choose orange-colored fruits..."*
*   *"Don't stress out..."*
*   *"...try replacing it with eggs..."*

While the author sounds caring (**considerate**) and sure (**confident**), the primary goal is not just to empathize or present objective data (**objective**), but to convince readers to take action for better health. This strong drive to influence behavior is what defines the text's persuasive tone.
