A title serves as the heading of an essay, representing the entire content of a text. A good title must meet several requirements, including:
- Reflecting the overall content of the text.
- Being in the form of a phrase (short and concise).
- Being attractive, logical, and specific.

The text as a whole discusses how the agricultural sector supports the economy, contributes to the Gross Domestic Product (GDP), survives during crises, and becomes the backbone driving the wheels of the economy in Indonesia.

Therefore, the most appropriate, specific title that can summarize all the main ideas in the text is **The Role of the Agricultural Sector in Indonesia**. The other options are either too broad, in the form of a full sentence, or only represent a small fraction of the text's overall content.
