To determine the expansion of a basic sentence, we must look for a sentence that has the exact same core elements (subject and predicate) as the original sentence.

The basic sentence in the question is **"PSBB began to be implemented"**. The core elements are:
- **Subject:** PSBB
- **Predicate:** began to be implemented

The sentence "PSBB began to be implemented in several regions in Indonesia due to the spike in Covid-19 patients" is the correct expansion. It fully retains the core elements and only adds a place adjunct ("in several regions in Indonesia") and a reason adjunct ("due to the spike in Covid-19 patients") without changing its basic structure. The other options have different main subjects or predicates.
