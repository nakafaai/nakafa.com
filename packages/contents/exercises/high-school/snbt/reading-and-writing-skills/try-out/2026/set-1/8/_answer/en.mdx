The word _resembles_ in sentence <InlineMath math="(5)" /> means "looks like" or "has a similar appearance to". In the context of the sentence, the base shape of the sarcophagus is described as having the same form as a boat.

The most appropriate synonymous word to replace _resembles_ without changing the original meaning of the sentence is _matching_. The other word choices are incorrect because _imitating_, _copying_, and _following the example of_ refer more to conscious actions or behaviors performed by an active subject (usually human), while _rivaling_ carries a connotation of competition.
