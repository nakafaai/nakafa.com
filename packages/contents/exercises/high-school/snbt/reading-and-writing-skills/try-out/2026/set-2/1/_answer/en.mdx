The punctuation error is found in sentence <InlineMath math="(12)" />.

In that sentence, there is a direct quotation followed by an attribution tag (*said Shanti*). According to punctuation rules, the comma separating a direct quote from its dialogue tag must be placed **before** the closing quotation mark, not after it.

Incorrect punctuation in the text:
*"... for one to two hours only", said Shanti.*

Correct punctuation:
*"... for one to two hours only," said Shanti.*
