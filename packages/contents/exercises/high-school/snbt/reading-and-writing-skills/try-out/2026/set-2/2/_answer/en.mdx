The *incomplete* sentence in paragraph <InlineMath math="(2)" /> is sentence <InlineMath math="(11)" />.

Sentence <InlineMath math="(11)" /> reads: "In addition, it must also be controlled and must not be left free to use gadgets as they please."

This sentence is incomplete because it does not have a clear **subject**. The phrases "must also be controlled" and "must not be left free" function as predicates, but there is no explicitly stated subject indicating who or what must be controlled in that sentence. Therefore, this sentence is missing one of the mandatory elements to be considered a complete and standard sentence.
