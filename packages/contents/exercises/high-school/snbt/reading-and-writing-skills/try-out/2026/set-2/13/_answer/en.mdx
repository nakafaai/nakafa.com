The appropriate conjunction to complete the blank part in sentence <InlineMath math="(7)" /> is **so**.

The conjunction "so" is used to express a consequence or result of a previously mentioned action or situation. In this sentence, the development of industrial sectors that are not based on existing resources results in the development of this sector being highly vulnerable to fluctuations occurring abroad. Therefore, the conjunction "so" is the most appropriate choice to logically connect the two parts of the sentence.
