The appropriate conjunction to complete the missing part in sentence <InlineMath math="(2)" /> is the word *that*.

The word *revealed* is a transitive verb that requires an object. Because the accompanying object is in the form of a clause ("he was enthusiastic..."), the conjunction *that* is highly necessary as a connecting word (clarifier) between the predicate and the subordinate clause that acts as the object.
