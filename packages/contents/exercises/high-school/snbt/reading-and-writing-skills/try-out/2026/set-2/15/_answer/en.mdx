A conclusion is a final statement or opinion of a text that encompasses the entirety of the ideas presented. The text as a whole discusses the large role, potential, and resilience of the agricultural sector for the national economy, even though it has not received the proper attention compared to other industrial sectors.

Thus, the most appropriate and logical conclusion to summarize all the ideas in the text is the assertion that agriculture is a sector that must be a priority for development in Indonesia.
