Diction refers to the precise and appropriate choice of words used to express an idea. The use of words in a sentence must adhere to standard grammatical rules.

In the text, there is an inappropriate use of diction, specifically the phrase *resulted by* in sentence <InlineMath math="(4)" />. The verb *result* is typically intransitive and takes prepositions like *in* or *from* (e.g., "results from"). Therefore, it is grammatically incorrect to use it in the passive form "be resulted by". To make the sentence standard and effective, the phrase *resulted by* should be replaced with the proper passive verb phrase *caused by* or *attributed to*.
