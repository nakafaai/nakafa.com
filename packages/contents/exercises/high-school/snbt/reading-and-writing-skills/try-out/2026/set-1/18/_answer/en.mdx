The writing of the phrase **ninety-percent** in sentence <InlineMath math="(5)" /> is incorrect. In standard writing rules, hyphenating a number and the word "percent" is unnecessary and incorrect unless they form a compound modifier directly preceding a noun.

Additionally, in texts presenting numerical data or percentages, it is highly recommended to write the number using digits followed directly by the percent symbol for clarity and ease of reading.

Therefore, the most precise and standard revision is **<InlineMath math="90\%" />**.
