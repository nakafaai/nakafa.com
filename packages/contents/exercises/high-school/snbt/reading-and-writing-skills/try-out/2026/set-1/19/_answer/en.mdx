The author's purpose in a text can be determined by identifying the main idea and the dominant information presented.

In the text, the main idea discussed is the impact of the Covid-19 pandemic on the automotive market sector, specifically the drastic decline in both new and used car sales. This is reinforced by presenting facts and data from a used car dealer who experienced a significant drop in sales.

Thus, it can be concluded that the main purpose of the author presenting the text is to **explain the condition of the car business during the Covid-19 pandemic**.
