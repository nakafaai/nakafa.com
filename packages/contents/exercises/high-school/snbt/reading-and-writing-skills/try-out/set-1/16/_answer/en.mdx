The reference word _This_ in sentence <InlineMath math="(3)" /> ("_This_ does not only happen to new car sales...") is used to refer back to the information that has been explained in the previous sentence.

If we look at sentence <InlineMath math="(2)" />, the sentence reads: "This is because **vehicle sales** are currently experiencing a quite drastic decline."

Therefore, the context of "_This_" (which is experiencing a drastic decline) specifically refers to the subject in the previous sentence, namely **vehicle sales**.
