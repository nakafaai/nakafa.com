The inappropriately used word form in the text is found in sentence <InlineMath math="(10)" />, specifically the word _produce_.

Consider the following excerpt from sentence <InlineMath math="(10)" />: "...any pollutants that **produce** agricultural activities, such as the use of pesticides and fertilizers."

The use of the active verb _produce_ in the sentence makes its meaning ambiguous and illogical, because it implies that the subject "pollutants" is the actor carrying out or creating the "agricultural activities." However, the context of the sentence intends to explain that the pollutants are the product or result of agricultural activities.

Therefore, in order for the sentence to be logical and coherent, the active verb _produce_ should be changed to the passive verb **_produced by_**, making the intended meaning "pollutants produced by agricultural activities."
