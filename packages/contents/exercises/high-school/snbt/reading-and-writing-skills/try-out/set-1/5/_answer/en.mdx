A good text title should be in the form of a phrase, logical, engaging, and most importantly, capable of summarizing all the main ideas within the text.

The text comprehensively discusses the sarcophagus, starting from its definition as a solid stone burial coffin, its role and use during the megalithic tradition, the distribution of its discovery locations, to its connection with the social status of past societies.

Therefore, the title that best represents the entire content of the text without being too narrow or specific to just one paragraph is "Sarcophagus as a Tomb of the Megalithic Tradition". The other options are less appropriate because they only focus on a small part of the information in the text, such as highlighting the location in North Sumatra or merely discussing social strata.
