A standard sentence must have clear core elements, consisting of at least a subject and a predicate.

The weakness in the text is found in sentence <InlineMath math="(1)" /> because it does not have a clear subject. The use of the preposition "As" at the beginning of the sentence turns the first clause into an adverbial element. After the comma, the use of the inter-clausal conjunction "then" actually obscures the position of the phrase "global warming", which should act as the subject of the main clause. For the sentence to be effective, the conjunction "then" should be removed.
