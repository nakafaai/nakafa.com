A standard sentence must have clear core elements, consisting of at least a subject and a predicate.

The weakness in the text is found in sentence <InlineMath math="(1)" /> because it does not have a clear subject. The use of the preposition _Sebagai_ (As) at the beginning of the sentence turns the first clause into an adverbial element. After the comma, the use of the inter-clausal conjunction _maka_ (then/so) actually obscures the position of the phrase _global warming_, which should act as the subject of the main clause. For the sentence to be effective, the conjunction _maka_ should be removed.
