The spelling of loanwords must be adjusted according to standard Indonesian spelling guidelines based on the Big Indonesian Dictionary (KBBI).

The base word _produktif_ indeed ends with the letter _f_. However, when the word receives the loan suffix _-itas_ (derived from the English _-ity_, as in the word _productivity_), the letter _f_ changes to _v_. Therefore, the correct standard spelling correction for the word _produktifitas_ in sentence <InlineMath math="(6)" /> is _produktivitas_.
