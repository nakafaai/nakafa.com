While the word *productiveness* can technically refer to the state of being productive, it is considered awkward and non-standard in this context.

When discussing the yield, efficiency, or output of a sector such as agriculture, the standard and formal term used is *productivity*. Therefore, to make sentence <InlineMath math="(6)" /> more natural and precise, the word *productiveness* should be corrected to *productivity*.
