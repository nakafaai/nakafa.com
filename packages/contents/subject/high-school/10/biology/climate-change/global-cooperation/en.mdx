## The Atmosphere Has No National Border

Greenhouse gases mix in the global atmosphere. That means emissions in one region can affect risk elsewhere. Global cooperation is needed for shared targets, funding, technology, and protection for vulnerable groups.

UNFCCC explains the Paris Agreement goal to limit global temperature rise and strengthen the climate response in [The Paris Agreement](https://unfccc.int/process-and-meetings/the-paris-agreement).

## From Global to Local

Global cooperation does not stop at conferences. Energy, transport, food, forest, school, and city decisions all shape whether targets become real action.

BMKG provides Indonesian climate information that supports monitoring and preparedness through [BMKG Climate Information](https://www.bmkg.go.id/iklim/).

## Self-Check

Why can climate change not be solved by one country alone?

Because greenhouse gases mix in the global atmosphere, impacts cross borders, and each country has different capacity for mitigation and adaptation.
