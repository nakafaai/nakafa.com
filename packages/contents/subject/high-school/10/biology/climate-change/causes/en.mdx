## The Problem Is in the Energy Budget

The greenhouse effect is the process where certain atmospheric gases absorb and re-emit infrared radiation. This natural process keeps Earth habitable, but extra greenhouse gases from human activity strengthen warming.

NOAA explains rising atmospheric carbon dioxide and its link to human activity in [Climate Change: Atmospheric Carbon Dioxide](https://www.climate.gov/news-features/understanding-climate/climate-change-atmospheric-carbon-dioxide).

## Natural and Human Causes Must Be Separated

Volcanoes, solar variation, and natural processes do affect climate. But rapid warming since the industrial era is mainly explained by increased greenhouse gases from human activity.

IPCC AR6 WG1 concludes that human influence has warmed the atmosphere, ocean, and land in the [Summary for Policymakers](https://www.ipcc.ch/report/ar6/wg1/).

## Self-Check

Why is the natural greenhouse effect not the same as climate change today?

The natural greenhouse effect keeps Earth warm. Today's problem is the strengthened effect caused by extra greenhouse gases from human activity.
