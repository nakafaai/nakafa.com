## Greenhouse gases change Earth's energy balance

The natural greenhouse effect helps keep Earth habitable. The problem appears when greenhouse gas concentration rises and more heat is retained. The Indonesian grade 10 science textbook emphasizes carbon dioxide, methane, fossil fuels, land-use change, refrigerants, vehicles, plastics, and organic waste. NASA explains the human role in increasing greenhouse gases in [Climate Change Causes](https://science.nasa.gov/climate-change/causes/).

## There is not only one cause

Modern climate change comes from combined activities that increase greenhouse gases and reduce carbon sinks. Because causes are spread across energy, transport, land, food, and waste, the discussion must be systemic. One habit change helps, but large change needs policy, technology, and changes in production.

## Self-check

Why is the greenhouse effect not the same as climate change?

Answer: the greenhouse effect is a natural heat-retention mechanism, while climate change occurs when that mechanism strengthens and shifts long-term climate patterns.

Explanation: without the greenhouse effect Earth would be too cold, but added greenhouse gases retain more heat.
