## Classification Makes Diversity Readable

Classification groups living organisms by similarities and differences. Without classification, organisms become a long list of names that is hard to study.

OpenStax Biology 2e introduces taxonomy as the science of grouping and naming organisms in [Organizing Life on Earth](https://openstax.org/books/biology-2e/pages/20-1-organizing-life-on-earth).

## The Chosen Trait Must Matter

Body color alone is often not enough for classification. Body structure, reproduction, genetic material, and evolutionary relationships provide stronger evidence.

## Self-Check

Why are two animals that both live in water not automatically close relatives?

Because habitat is not the only basis for classification. Body traits, reproduction, and evolutionary relationships also need to be checked.
