# Nakafa Trademark and Brand Policy

Effective date: April 24, 2026

PT. Nakafa Tekno Kreatif owns the Nakafa brand. Public source code access does
not grant permission to use Nakafa names, logos, domains, or visual identity.

## 1. Definitions

`Nakafa brand assets` means the names, marks, visual identity, and source
identifiers covered by this policy.

`You` means the individual or entity using or referring to Nakafa brand assets.

`Written permission` means a separate written agreement signed by PT. Nakafa
Tekno Kreatif, or a written approval sent from an official Nakafa email address.

## 2. Protected Brand Assets

This policy covers:

- The names `Nakafa`, `Nakafa AI`, `Nakafa School`, and related product names.
- Nakafa logos, icons, marks, badges, screenshots, trade dress, UI identity,
  color identity, and visual style when used as source-identifying brand assets.
- Domains, subdomains, social handles, email names, package names, project
  names, and organization names that include or imitate Nakafa.
- Any confusingly similar name, logo, domain, product identity, or brand
  presentation.

## 3. Allowed References

You may use the word `Nakafa` in plain text only when the reference is truthful,
limited, and not confusing.

Allowed examples:

- Referring to the official Nakafa project.
- Linking to `https://nakafa.com`.
- Saying that you found an issue in Nakafa.
- Saying that your contribution was submitted to Nakafa.

## 4. Permission Required

You must get prior written permission from PT. Nakafa Tekno Kreatif before using
any Nakafa brand asset in a product, service, domain, social account, repository,
course, school deployment, company deployment, marketing page, presentation,
fundraising material, advertisement, or public announcement.

The following uses are not allowed without written permission:

- Calling your project, product, fork, service, course, school system, or company
  `Nakafa` or a confusingly similar name.
- Using Nakafa logos, icons, screenshots, UI identity, or visual identity as your
  own branding.
- Using Nakafa marks in a domain, subdomain, package name, app name, repository
  name, organization name, social handle, or email address.
- Saying or implying that your project is official, endorsed, sponsored,
  partnered, certified, maintained, or approved by Nakafa.
- Rebranding Nakafa as your own product.
- Removing Nakafa attribution from Nakafa materials.

## 5. Forks, Modifications, and Separate Agreements

The source code license does not grant permission to distribute forks,
modifications, or derivative products.

If PT. Nakafa Tekno Kreatif gives you separate written permission to make or
distribute a modified version, that permission does not include Nakafa brand
rights unless the written agreement explicitly says so.

Modified versions must not create confusion with official Nakafa services.

## 6. Governing Law and Venue

This policy is governed by the laws of the Republic of Indonesia, without regard
to conflict of law principles.

Except where prohibited by applicable law, any dispute arising out of or related
to this policy or Nakafa brand assets must be brought exclusively in the courts
of the Republic of Indonesia.

## 7. No Waiver

If PT. Nakafa Tekno Kreatif does not enforce a term immediately, that does not
waive the right to enforce that term later.

Any waiver must be in writing and signed by PT. Nakafa Tekno Kreatif.

## 8. Severability

If any part of this policy is found unenforceable, the rest remains in effect.
The unenforceable part will be interpreted as narrowly as possible so it becomes
enforceable while preserving the original intent.
